### Key Principles

1. *Sustaining of General Authorities, Area Seventies, and General Officers - President Dallin H. Oaks:* Common consent unites us in covenant loyalty to living prophets and the Lord’s house of order.
2. *Church Auditing Department Report, 2024 - Jared B. Larson:* Faithful, provident stewardship of sacred funds reflects consecration, integrity, and trust in God’s revealed pattern.
3. *As a Little Child - President Jeffrey R. Holland:* Childlike humility, purity, and trust anchor discipleship and priesthood service in Christlike love.
4. *Spiritually Whole in Him - President Camille N. Johnson:* Through faith, repentance, and covenants, Christ grants spiritual wholeness now while we patiently await full healing.
5. *Right Before Our Eyes - Elder Ronald A. Rasband:* As the Lord hastens His work in temples and missions, we hasten our holiness by worshiping and trusting Jesus Christ.
6. *The Atonement of Jesus Christ Provides the Ultimate Rescue - Elder Quentin L. Cook:* The Savior’s Atonement is our refuge and model, moving us to rescue others and remember Him in sacrament and temple.
7. *My Love for the Savior Is My “Why” - Elder Ricardo P. Giménez:* Love for Jesus Christ is the covenant-centered motive that turns prophetic invitations into joyful discipleship.
8. *“Draw Near unto Me” - President Henry B. Eyring:* Daily prayer, scripture, sacrament, and service invite the Spirit and draw us closer to the Savior in our homes and families.
9. *Cherishing Life - Elder Neil L. Andersen:* Disciples honor the sanctity of life with Christlike compassion, moral courage, and trust in the Savior’s healing Atonement.
10. *Divine Authority, Sublime Young Men - President Steven J. Lund:* Aaronic Priesthood authority prepares young men to bless the Church through sacred ordinances—especially the sacrament—in covenant power.
11. *“Return unto Me … That I May Heal You” - Elder S. Mark Palmer:* However far we’ve strayed, Christ lovingly welcomes our return through repentance, ordinances, and nurturing fellowship.
12. *Faith: A Bond of Trust and Loyalty - Elder Sandino Roman:* Faith matures from trusting Christ to loyal covenant keeping that invites His power and miracles in God’s time.
13. *Personal Preparation to Meet the Savior - Elder Dale G. Renlund:* Simple obedience, Spirit-guided living, and service to “the least of these” calm fear and prepare us for His coming.
14. *True to the Faith That Our Parents Have Cherished - Elder Hans T. Boom:* Drawing strength from faithful forebears, we build on Christ’s rock, keep covenants, and join the gathering through missionary service.
15. *“By This All Will Know That You Are My Disciples” - Elder Dieter F. Uchtdorf:* Charity-fueled belonging and unity are the Church’s living witness that we are Christ’s disciples.
16. *And We Talk of Christ - Elder Gary E. Stevenson:* A Christ-centered life speaks of, worships, and testifies of the living, resurrected Lord—especially in a holier Easter.
17. *Thou Art the Christ - Sister Amy A. Wright:* In home and Primary, we help children believe, belong through covenants, and become like Jesus Christ.
18. *The Plan of Mercy - Elder James R. Rasband:* God’s plan is a plan of mercy accessed through temples, repentance, and the Book of Mormon’s witness of Christ.
19. *Our Heavenly Guidance System - Elder Sergio R. Vargas:* The Light of Christ and covenants steady our course so we endure—and rejoice—to the end in Jesus.
20. *Worship - Elder D. Todd Christofferson:* True worship yields our hearts to the Father and the Son, shaping daily discipleship through covenants and the Holy Ghost.
21. *The Times of Restitution of All Things - Elder David A. Bednar:* The Restoration’s divine sequence—First Vision to priesthood keys—summons us to seek personal witness and build Zion.
22. *Participate to Prepare for Christ’s Return - Elder Steven D. Shumway:* Saying yes to callings and service is the Lord’s way to steadily prepare us and our families to meet Him.
23. *Your Repentance Doesn’t Burden Jesus Christ; It Brightens His Joy - Sister Tamara W. Runia:* Repentance is a hopeful gift that affirms our unchanging worth and deepens our covenant bond with Christ.
24. *Compensating Blessings - Bishop Gérald Caussé:* When righteous desires are blocked, the Lord—through His love and Atonement—provides compensating blessings as we consecrate all we can.
25. *Eternity’s Great Gifts: Jesus Christ’s Atonement, Resurrection, Restoration - Elder Gerrit W. Gong:* In Jesus Christ we find peace, becoming, and belonging that gather us into covenant families and His restored Church.
26. *Joy Through Covenant Discipleship - Elder John A. McCune:* Binding ourselves to Christ through covenants brings power, joy, and an eternal perspective that turns us outward to gather Israel.
27. *Divine Helps for Mortality - President Dallin H. Oaks:* Through Christ’s Atonement, the Light of Christ, commandments, covenants, and the Holy Ghost, God equips us to grow through opposition.
28. *Reverence for Sacred Things - Elder Ulisses Soares:* Reverence unlocks revelation, deepens joy, safeguards covenants, and elevates worship at home, in sacrament, and in the temple.
29. *Charity—a Sign of True Discipleship - Elder Michael B. Strong:* Becoming like Christ is measured by active charity that notices needs and lifts others along the covenant path.
30. *Beware the Second Temptation - Elder Scott D. Whiting:* After sin, healing comes as we run to Christ and His covenant community rather than hiding in shame.
31. *Harden Not Your Heart - Elder Christopher H. Kim:* Daily repentance and humility soften our hearts to receive the Spirit’s light and understanding.
32. *Receive His Gift - Elder Patrick Kearon:* Embracing our divine identity and receiving the Father’s supreme Gift—His Son—opens the way to every other heavenly gift.
33. *The Love of God - Elder Benjamin M. Z. Tai:* Christ embodies the Father’s perfect, personal, perpetual love, felt through faith, repentance, covenants, and Spirit-led service.
34. *Confidence in the Presence of God - President Russell M. Nelson:* Charity, virtue, and temple-centered covenant living cause our confidence before God to wax strong now and eternally.
