### Summary

Elder David A. Bednar testifies that Jesus Christ has restored His gospel and Church in the latter days, outlining the divine sequence of the First Vision, the Book of Mormon, priesthood authority and keys, the formal organization of the Church, and our role in the dispensation of the fulness of times, and inviting all to seek a personal witness by the Holy Ghost.

### Haiku

Morning grove light falls—  
Keys, covenants rise again;  
Stone fills all the earth.

### Key Points

1) The First Vision begins the Restoration  
- God the Father and Jesus Christ appeared to Joseph Smith, clarifying the nature of the Godhead and inaugurating the “restitution of all things,” with Joseph seeking to know truth so he could do it.

2) Scripture and priesthood restored by heavenly messengers  
- The Book of Mormon came forth as another testament of Jesus Christ, confirming the Bible and restoring plain and precious truths; John the Baptist and then Peter, James, and John restored priesthood authority and keys, later expanded in the Kirtland Temple by Moses, Elias, and Elijah—including the sealing power.

3) The Church organized and the work rolls forth in the fulness of times  
- With scripture and priesthood established, the Church was organized on April 6, 1830; this last dispensation gathers all things in Christ as the gospel rolls forth like Daniel’s prophesied stone, preparing the world for the Second Coming and inviting each person to gain a personal witness and act in faith.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- Acts 3:21 — “The times of restitution of all things.”
- Joseph Smith—History 1:11–20 — The First Vision; “Hear Him.”
- Articles of Faith 1:8 — Bible and Book of Mormon as the word of God.
- 3 Nephi 11 — The resurrected Christ’s ministry in the Americas.
- Doctrine and Covenants 13 — Restoration of the Aaronic Priesthood by John the Baptist.
- Doctrine and Covenants 27:12–13 — Peter, James, and John restore the Melchizedek Priesthood and keys.
- Doctrine and Covenants 110:11–16 — Moses (gathering of Israel), Elias (Abrahamic covenant), Elijah (sealing power).
- Doctrine and Covenants 128:18 — The welding of dispensations in the fulness of times.
- Daniel 2:34–35, 44–45 — Stone cut without hands filling the earth.
- Ephesians 1:10 — Gathering all things in Christ.
- Gospel principles: priesthood authority versus priesthood keys; covenants and ordinances of salvation; gathering Israel on both sides of the veil; the centrality of the Atonement and Resurrection; personal revelation that leads to action.

- In what specific ways have priesthood authority and keys blessed your covenant path and your family?
