### Key Principles

1. Members reaffirm their support for Church leaders, underscoring unity and collective recognition of divine leadership. ([&ldquo;Sustaining of General Authorities, Area Seventies, and General Officers&rdquo; - Presented by President Henry B. Eyring](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/11eyring?lang=eng))
2. Hope is a divine gift that grows with faith in Jesus Christ, sustaining us through trials. ([&ldquo;The Triumph of Hope&rdquo; - Elder Neil L. Andersen](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/12andersen?lang=eng))
3. Participation in priesthood ordinances and covenants connects us to divine power. ([&ldquo;Live Up to Your Privileges&rdquo; - President Emily Belle Freeman](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/13freeman?lang=eng))
4. Recognizing and accepting God's love enhances our resilience and joy. ([&ldquo;God’s Favourite&rdquo; - Elder Karl D. Hirst](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/14hirst?lang=eng))
5. The gospel and Church of Jesus Christ offer transformative spiritual power. ([&ldquo;“This Is My Gospel”—“This Is My Church”&rdquo; - Elder Dale G. Renlund](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/15renlund?lang=eng))
6. Trusting God in all aspects of life leads to peace and alignment with eternal truths. ([&ldquo;Trusting Our Father&rdquo; - Elder David P. Homer](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/16homer?lang=eng))
7. We are called to serve others and bring them closer to Christ. ([&ldquo;God Loves All His Children&rdquo; - Elder Gregorio E. Casillas](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/17casillas?lang=eng))
8. Following Christ's teachings fosters love and peacemaking. ([&ldquo;Following Christ&rdquo; - President Dallin H. Oaks](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/18oaks?lang=eng))
9. Surrendering rebellion to God deepens our conversion and joy. ([&ldquo;Burying Our Weapons of Rebellion&rdquo; - Elder D. Todd Christofferson](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/21christofferson?lang=eng))
10. Remaining connected to Christ helps us spiritually enrich our lives and others'. ([&ldquo;Bonded to Jesus Christ: Becoming the Salt of the Earth&rdquo; - Elder José A. Teixeira](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/22teixeira?lang=eng))
11. Christ is ever-present to help us when we reach out in faith. ([&ldquo;His Hand Ready to Help Us&rdquo; - Elder Juan Pablo Villar](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/23villar?lang=eng))
12. Embrace joy as central to gospel living and church worship. ([&ldquo;Welcome to the Church of Joy&rdquo; - Elder Patrick Kearon](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/24kearon?lang=eng))
13. Building high and holy relationships mirrors the Savior's friendship. ([&ldquo;“Ye Are My Friends”&rdquo; - Elder David L. Buckner](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/25buckner?lang=eng))
14. Daily repentance maintains spiritual cleanliness and receptivity to the Holy Ghost. ([&ldquo;Be Thou Clean&rdquo; - Elder D. Martin Goury](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/26goury?lang=eng))
15. Service and support can guide others to receive God's blessings. ([&ldquo;The Wind Did Never Cease to Blow&rdquo; - Elder Aroldo B. Cavalcante](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/27cavalcante?lang=eng))
16. Aligning our will with God's helps us attain eternal treasures. ([&ldquo;Aligning Our Will with His&rdquo; - Elder Ulisses Soares](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/28soares?lang=eng))
17. Integrating holiness in daily activities brings joy and closeness to God. ([&ldquo;Holiness to the Lord in Everyday Life&rdquo; - Elder Gerrit W. Gong](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/31gong?lang=eng))
18. The Savior's atonement redeems us, transforming weaknesses into strength. ([&ldquo;The Joy of Our Redemption&rdquo; - Sister Kristin M. Yee](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/32yee?lang=eng))
19. Joseph Smith's role in the Restoration is pivotal and invites continual revelation. ([&ldquo;The Man Who Communed with Jehovah&rdquo; - Elder Kyle S. McKay](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/33mckay?lang=eng))
20. Continual repentance empowers immediate joy and spiritual growth. ([&ldquo;Embrace the Lord’s Gift of Repentance&rdquo; - Elder Jorge M. Alvarado](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/34alvarado?lang=eng))
21. Pride can turn divine prosperity into a distraction; remain humble and faithful. ([&ldquo;In the Space of Not Many Years&rdquo; - Elder David A. Bednar](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/35bednar?lang=eng))
22. Christ's charity exemplifies divine obedience calling us to follow His teachings. ([&ldquo;“I Am He”&rdquo; - President Jeffrey R. Holland](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/41holland?lang=eng))
23. Sincere questions lead to growth through faith-led learning. ([&ldquo;Seeking Answers to Spiritual Questions&rdquo; - Sister Tracy Y. Browning](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/42browning?lang=eng))
24. Heavenly Father's plan through mortality is designed for our success. ([&ldquo;Mortality Works!&rdquo; - Elder Brook P. Hales](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/43hales?lang=eng))
25. Regular communion with God enriches worship and spiritual strength. ([&ldquo;Seek Him with All Your Heart&rdquo; - Bishop L. Todd Budge](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/45budge?lang=eng))
26. Anticipate fulfilling opportunities to share the gospel through service. ([&ldquo;Days Never to Be Forgotten&rdquo; - Elder Gary E. Stevenson](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/44stevenson?lang=eng))
27. Youth are trusted with divine responsibilities in gathering Israel. ([&ldquo;O Youth of the Noble Birthright&rdquo; - Brother Bradley R. Wilcox](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/46wilcox?lang=eng))
28. Teaching and living the simple doctrines of Christ fortify spiritual safety. ([&ldquo;Simple Is the Doctrine of Jesus Christ&rdquo; - President Henry B. Eyring](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/47eyring?lang=eng))
29. Rooting our faith deep in Christ naturally strengthens our testimonies. ([&ldquo;Nourish the Roots, and the Branches Will Grow&rdquo; - Elder Dieter F. Uchtdorf](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/51uchtdorf?lang=eng))
30. Embrace the guidance of scriptures and the Holy Ghost to fulfill divine potential. ([&ldquo;The Words of Christ and the Holy Ghost Will Lead Us to the Truth&rdquo; - Elder Takashi Wada](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/52wada?lang=eng))
31. Reflecting Christ through covenants and following the prophet holds up His light. ([&ldquo;“Behold I Am the Light Which Ye Shall Hold Up”&rdquo; - Elder Ronald A. Rasband](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/53rasband?lang=eng))
32. Scriptures are crucial for conversion and faithfulness in the gospel. ([&ldquo;Sacred Scriptures—the Foundations of Faith&rdquo; - Elder Quentin L. Cook](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/54cook?lang=eng))
33. We are literal children of God with the potential to become like Him. ([&ldquo;Sons and Daughters of God&rdquo; - Elder Rubén V. Alliaud](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/55alliaud?lang=eng))
34. Prioritizing Christ over distractions leads to true joy and success. ([&ldquo;Focus on Jesus Christ and His Gospel&rdquo; - Elder I. Raymond Egbo](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/56egbo?lang=eng))
35. Prepare for the Second Coming by prioritizing discipleship and temple worship. ([&ldquo;The Lord Jesus Christ Will Come Again&rdquo; - President Russell M. Nelson](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/10/57nelson?lang=eng))
