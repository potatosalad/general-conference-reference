### Summary

Elder Bednar discusses how pride can transform the blessing of prosperity into a curse, using the contrasting behaviors of the Nephites and Lamanites in the Book of Helaman as a warning for today.

### Haiku

Prosperity's snare,  
Pride's subtle, potent toxin—  
Guard hearts, seek Christ's light.

### Key Points

1. **Danger of Prosperity**: Prosperity can lead to pride, which causes spiritual blindness and diverts us from eternal truths if we are not careful.
   
2. **Contrast Between Nephites and Lamanites**: The Lamanites rose in righteousness as the Nephites fell into prideful apostasy, showing the consequences of pride and the blessings of faithfulness.

3. **Lessons from the Book of Mormon**: The Book of Mormon was written for our day to warn and teach us about pride and to help us remain humble and steadfast in our faith.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- Helaman 6:34-36: The spiritual upturn of the Lamanites and downturn of the Nephites.
- Moroni 10:27: Moroni's foreknowledge of modern-day behaviors.
- Doctrine of Pride: Pride as a spiritual danger leading to self-focus and forgetting God.

### Questions to Ponder

- How does prosperity in my life influence my relationship with God?
- What ways can I guard against pride in my daily living?
- How do the example of the Lamanites inspire a change of heart in my own spiritual journey?
- In what ways have I seen pride affect a community or group, and what lessons can be learned?
- How can the teachings of the Book of Mormon help me navigate today's challenges?
