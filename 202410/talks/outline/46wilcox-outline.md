### Summary

Brother Bradley R. Wilcox encourages the youth of the Church to embrace their birthright and responsibilities as covenant children of God, emphasizing their role in helping bring others to Christ with love and trust from Heavenly Father.

### Haiku

Youth of noble birth,  
Trust in sacred covenants,  
Different from the world.

### Key Points

1. **The Importance of the Birthright**: Brother Wilcox explains that as covenant children, members of the Church have a special birthright that includes responsibilities and blessings, setting them apart to serve and lead others to Christ.

2. **Living Differently is a Trust**: The difference in lifestyle as Latter-day Saints is not about being better, but about being trusted by God to lead His children home, emphasizing the learning and growth that comes from those responsibilities.

3. **Divine Assistance and Partnership with Christ**: As youth fulfill their responsibilities, they work hand-in-hand with the Savior, receiving divine assistance and strength, which helps them to overcome worldly challenges and fulfill their divine potential.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- **2 Nephi 2:6**: The role of Jesus Christ as the source of redemption through His grace and truth.
- **Joint-heirs with Christ**: Understanding our potential to inherit divine attributes and blessings.
- **Covenant Relationship**: The power and protection that come through making and keeping sacred covenants.

### Questions to Ponder

- How can I embrace the trust that God has placed in me as a youth of the noble birthright?
- In what ways can I strengthen my covenant relationship with Christ to better receive His divine assistance?
- How do I view my responsibilities differently when I consider them as opportunities for growth rather than obligations?
- What does it mean to be set apart in the world, and how can I use that distinction to serve others?

These questions can help deepen understanding and reflection on the principles taught by Brother Wilcox, fostering a meaningful class discussion.
