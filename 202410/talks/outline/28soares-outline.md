### Summary

Elder Ulisses Soares emphasizes the importance of aligning our desires and will with God’s will to obtain the eternal treasures of His kingdom.

### Haiku

Precious pearl of light,  
Align our will with the Lord's,  
Eternal life gained.

### Key Points

1. **The Pursuit of Eternal Treasure**: Like the merchant seeking the pearl of great price, we must prioritize the kingdom of heaven over worldly possessions or desires.

2. **Submission to God's Will**: We should follow Christ's example of aligning our will completely with the Father’s, seeking to do what pleases God above our personal preferences.

3. **Avoiding Self-Centeredness**: Rather than living by "our truth," we should seek to do what is truly Christlike, which often requires rejecting worldly philosophies and influences.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- **Parable of the Pearl of Great Price**: Matthew 13:45-46
- **Christ’s Submission to God's Will**: John 6:38-40, John 8:29
- **Teachings on Selflessness**: 1 Corinthians 2:16, Philippians 2:21
- **Elder Quentin L. Cook's Teachings on Being Christlike**

### Questions to Ponder

- What “pearls” or treasures in your life may be keeping you from fully committing to God’s kingdom?
- How can you better align your daily decisions with what pleases the Lord?
- In what ways might we be tempted to follow personal desires over divine guidance, and how can we overcome these temptations?
