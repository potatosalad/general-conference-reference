### Summary

Elder Patrick Kearon invites us to embrace joy in our worship, especially during sacrament meetings, as we are members of the church of joy centered on Jesus Christ.

### Haiku

Joy in Christ we find,  
In sacrament we’re renewed,  
Worship with glad heart.

### Key Points

1. **Joyful Worship**: Elder Kearon emphasizes that members of the Church should experience and express joy in every aspect of their worship, particularly during sacrament meetings, transforming these gatherings into joyful, Christ-centered experiences.

2. **Reverence with Joy**: He challenges the notion that reverence is solely about silence and stillness, advocating for a "joyful reverence" that reflects a soul rejoicing in Christ’s love and salvation.

3. **Sacrament Purpose**: Elder Kearon redefines the sacrament as a time for spiritual renewal and gratitude rather than guilt, encouraging reflection on Christ's love, His sacrifice, and the joy of redemption.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- **Psalm 16:11** - "In thy presence is fulness of joy."
- **Alma 36:21** - Reflects on the joy that comes from Jesus Christ.
- **3 Nephi 18:11** - The purpose and sacredness of the sacrament.
- **President Russell M. Nelson's teachings** - Joy through Jesus Christ as shared in multiple addresses.
