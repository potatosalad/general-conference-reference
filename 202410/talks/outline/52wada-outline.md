### Summary

Elder Takashi Wada teaches that by feasting on the words of Christ and listening to the guidance of the Holy Ghost, we can find direction and purpose in life, recognizing that we are children of God with divine potential.

### Haiku

Words of Christ guide us,  
Holy Ghost whispers the truth,  
Path to God revealed.

### Key Points

1. **Identity and Divine Potential**: Understanding that we are spirit children of Heavenly Father gives us insight into our divine potential and purpose in life.
   
2. **Guidance through the Words of Christ**: Feasting upon the scriptures, especially the words of Christ, provides direction, comfort, and hope during life's challenges.
   
3. **Personal Revelation through the Holy Ghost**: Sincere prayer invites the Holy Ghost to confirm truth and provide personal guidance and peace, helping us feel Heavenly Father's love.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- **2 Nephi 32:3, 5**: Feasting upon the words of Christ and the guidance of the Holy Ghost.
- **Matthew 11:28-30**: Christ’s invitation to find rest by coming unto Him.
- **Prayer as taught by the Savior**: Importance of personal prayer in building a relationship with God.
- **The Plan of Salvation**: Understanding our pre-mortal life and eternal potential.
