### Summary

Elder Ronald A. Rasband emphasizes the importance of holding up the light of Jesus Christ by adhering to covenants and supporting the living prophet.

### Haiku

Hold up the Savior,  
Light and love in our journey—  
Covenants will guide.

### Key Points

1. **Sustaining Leaders**: Elder Rasband explains that sustaining Church leaders, including the prophet, involves actively defending, following their counsel, and praying for them.
2. **Covenants and Temples**: He highlights the importance of keeping covenants made in temples, which connect us to the Savior and provide spiritual strength.
3. **Personal Revelation and Light**: Holding up Christ's light allows us to receive personal revelation and divine assistance, crucial in a world filled with challenges.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- **1 Nephi 17:3** - Emphasizes the Lord’s support through challenges.
- **Mosiah 18:8-9** - Encouragement to bear one another’s burdens.
- **Doctrine and Covenants 10:5** - The need for constant prayer to overcome Satan.
- **Matthew 5:14-16** - Letting your light shine as a beacon to others.

### Questions to Ponder

- How can we more effectively support our Church leaders and local Church members?
- What specific actions can we take to hold up the light of Christ in our daily lives?
- In what ways have temple covenants strengthened and guided us in times of challenge?
- How does personal revelation play a role in our journey to hold up the light of Christ?
