### Summary

Elder Quentin L. Cook emphasizes the crucial role of sacred scriptures, such as the Bible and the Book of Mormon, in guiding individuals to conversion and steadfastness in the gospel of Jesus Christ.

### Haiku

Sacred texts guide us,  
Foundations of faith and truth,  
Firm against the storm.

### Key Points

1. **Role of Scriptures in Conversion**  
   Elder Cook highlights how sacred scriptures are essential tools for conversion, providing spiritual guidance and doctrinal direction that strengthen faith and testimony.

2. **Complementarity of the Bible and Book of Mormon**  
   He explains that the Bible and the Book of Mormon complement each other, joining together as one in the Lord's hand to provide a richer and more complete understanding of the gospel.

3. **Navigating Modern Challenges**  
   Elder Cook advises against negative online influences, advocating instead for intentional consumption of digital content that upholds faith and encourages consistent engagement with the scriptures.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- **2 Timothy 3:16-17** - All scripture is given for teaching and guidance.
- **Book of Mormon, Alma 42** - Explains the Atonement and the importance of mortality's probationary purpose.
- **Ezekiel 37:16-17** - Prophecy of the Bible and the Book of Mormon being one in the Lord's hand.
- **Moroni 10:3-5** - Encouragement to read, ponder, and pray about the Book of Mormon.
