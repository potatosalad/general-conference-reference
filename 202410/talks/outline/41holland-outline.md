### Summary
President Jeffrey R. Holland taught about Christ's unwavering charity, emphasizing that His obedience and love during His trials illustrate His divine mission and inspire us to follow His commandments more fully.

### Haiku

Love in suffering,  
Christ's pure charity endures,  
Obey and be true.

### Key Points

1. **Divine Presence and Authority**: Even without worldly attributes like wealth or beauty, Christ's presence was powerful and authoritative, demonstrating divine influence and identity.
2. **True Love and Commandments**: Christlike love is shown through complete obedience to God's will. Understanding and living this love helps us align with divine truth and grace.
3. **Persistent Faith in Trials**: Despite opposition, Christ's example of unwavering faith and obedience amidst trials is a model for us to persist in our faith.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- **John 18:4-6**: Antagonists fall at Christ’s declaration, "I Am He," illustrating His divine authority.
- **Isaiah 53:2**: Prophecy of Christ having "no form nor comeliness" aligns with His humble earthly presence.
- **Lehi’s teaching (2 Nephi 2:6)**: "Redemption cometh in and through the Holy Messiah; for he is full of grace and truth."
- **Commandments of Love**: The instructions to love God and others with all one’s heart (Matthew 22:36-40).

### Questions to Ponder

- How can I better exhibit Christlike love in my obedience to God's commandments?
- In what ways can I recognize and respond to the divine authority of Christ in my life?
- How can I persist in faith during trials or when faced with opposition to my beliefs?
- What specific actions can I take to deepen my love for God and my neighbor in everyday life?
