### Summary

Elder Juan Pablo Villar emphasizes that as we reach out to Jesus Christ in faith, He will always be there to help us overcome challenges and adversities.

### Haiku

Faith extends our hands,  
Christ's rescue always close by,  
Waves meet His embrace.

### Key Points

1. **The Savior’s Power and Presence**  
   Jesus Christ has power over all adversities; when we reach out in faith, He will grasp our hands and guide us to safety.

2. **Christ’s Ministering Example**  
   We should follow Christ’s example by not only being present but by actively ministering and providing necessary aid to those in need, relying on the Holy Ghost for guidance.

3. **The Savior’s Relentless Love**  
   Just as Elder Villar’s brother did not give up on him, the Savior will persistently be there to help each one of us as many times as needed, embodying His eternal love and sacrifice.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- Jesus calming the storm, Mark 4:39.
- Peter walking on water, Matthew 14:28-31.
- President Russell M. Nelson's teaching on celestial thinking.
- The Atonement of Jesus Christ.
- The power of ministering and serving others.

### Questions to Ponder

- How have you experienced the Savior reaching out to you in times of trial?
- In what ways can we minister to others like Christ does for us?
- What does it mean to you to think celestial when facing your personal challenges?
