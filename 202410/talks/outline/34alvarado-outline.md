### Summary

Elder Jorge M. Alvarado's talk emphasizes the vital importance of embracing the Lord's gift of repentance, encouraging an immediate and continuous turn to God, and highlighting that true joy comes through sincere repentance and faith in Jesus Christ.

### Haiku

Repentance brings joy,  
In Christ's strength we're forgiven,  
Turn now, hearts rejoice.

### Key Points

1. **Repentance and Renewal**: Elder Alvarado underscores that repentance is a joyful, ongoing process that leads to personal renewal and strength in Christ.
   
2. **Examples of Transformation**: By sharing personal and scriptural examples of transformation through repentance, Elder Alvarado highlights the boundless reach of Christ’s love and the power of restitution and personal change.
   
3. **Temple as a Goal**: Striving to be worthy of temple covenants is emphasized as a motivator and a culmination of sincere repentance, leading to personal holiness and closer unity with God.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- **2 Nephi 4:17-19**: Nephi's struggle and his reliance on the Lord.
- **Doctrine and Covenants 58:42**: Assurance of forgiveness through repentance.
- **Mosiah 4:10**: Encouragement to believe in God and repent of sins.
- **Teachings of President Russell M. Nelson** on the importance of temple worship.

- How can recognizing our feelings of inadequacy lead us to Christ more fully?
- What does daily repentance look like in our lives, and how can it bring joy?
- How does the temple, as a house of the Lord, motivate us to strive for spiritual purity and closer relationship with Jesus Christ?
