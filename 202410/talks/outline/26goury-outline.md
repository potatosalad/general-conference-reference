### Summary

Elder D. Martin Goury emphasized the importance of daily repentance to remain spiritually clean and better receive the promptings of the Holy Ghost, thereby aiding us in our spiritual journey.

### Haiku

Repent and be clean,  
A heart washed daily shines bright,  
Holy Ghost will guide.

### Key Points

1. **Importance of Repentance**: Repentance is a daily necessity that cleanses us spiritually, allowing us to be prepared to enter God's kingdom and receive His blessings.
   
2. **Guidance of the Holy Ghost**: Regular repentance enhances our ability to discern and follow the promptings of the Holy Ghost, which is crucial for spiritual survival and making righteous decisions.
   
3. **Practical Application**: By heeding the Holy Ghost's guidance, as demonstrated in practical examples, we can align with God's will and serve others effectively.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- **Alma 34:32-35**: Emphasizes the importance of not procrastinating repentance.
- **Helaman 4:24**: The Spirit of the Lord dwells not in unholy temples.
- **D&C 19:16-20**: The necessity and power of repentance and Christ’s Atonement.
- President Russell M. Nelson's teachings on daily repentance.
