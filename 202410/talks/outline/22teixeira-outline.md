### Summary

Elder José A. Teixeira emphasizes the importance of being bonded to Jesus Christ to become the "salt of the earth," enriching and preserving our spiritual vitality through service, meaningful worship, and living Christlike lives.

### Haiku

Salt of the earth shines,  
Bonded with Christ's pure light, we  
Serve with true purpose.

### Key Points

1. **Bonding with Christ**: Just as salt comprises two bonded elements, we must be bonded with Jesus Christ to truly become effective disciples and serve as the salt of the earth.

2. **Service and Faith**: Demonstrating unwavering dedication in service and mission work is vital to being a disciple of Christ and impacting the world positively.

3. **Intentional Actions**: Keeping temples central, strengthening others through gospel living, willingly serving in callings, and purposeful digital communication help maintain our spiritual vitality.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- **Matthew 5:13**: “Ye are the salt of the earth…”
- **Covenant and discipleship**: Being in a covenant with Christ to possess His qualities.
- **Elder Dieter F. Uchtdorf’s teachings on discipleship**: Reflect on talks about serving others and living the gospel.
- **The importance of temples**: Centering life around the temple and its ordinances.

### Questions to Ponder

- How can we ensure our daily actions reflect our bond with Jesus Christ?
- In what ways can we prevent our faith from becoming "casual" and losing its vitality?
- How can we utilize digital tools to serve and minister effectively while avoiding distractions?
- What specific steps can I take to ensure the temple remains central in my life?
