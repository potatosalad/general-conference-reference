### Summary

Elder Gerrit W. Gong emphasizes that making "Holiness to the Lord" a part of our everyday lives sanctifies our daily activities, brings us joy, and draws us closer to God and each other.

### Haiku

Holiness in life,  
Daily acts bring sacred joy,  
Closer to the Lord.

### Key Points

1. **The Importance of Holiness**: Elder Gong explains how holiness in everyday life makes our daily actions sacred and helps us become more like God.
2. **Living the Seven Cs**: He describes living in "communion, community, compassion, commitment, and covenant with God," centered in Jesus Christ, as a path toward greater happiness and fulfillment.
3. **Temple Worship and Holiness**: Regular temple attendance and participation in temple ordinances help reinforce holiness in our lives, acting as an anchor for our spiritual journey.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- **1 Peter 1:16** - "Be ye holy; for I am holy."
- **Doctrine and Covenants 138** - Teachings about the spirit world and the interconnections between this life and the next.
- **Alma 36** - A personal story of overcoming darkness through faith and feeling God's love.

### Questions to Ponder

- How can I infuse my daily routines with a sense of holiness and purpose?
- What steps can I take to strengthen my communion and commitment with God?
- How does regular temple attendance impact my sense of holiness and connection with the divine?
