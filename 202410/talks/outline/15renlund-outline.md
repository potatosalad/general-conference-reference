### Summary

Elder Dale G. Renlund explains that the combination of the Savior's gospel and His Church offers powerful and transformative blessings.

### Haiku

Gospel and the Church,  
Together they transform us,  
Power flows through them.

### Key Points

1. **Gospel and Church Synergy**: Elder Renlund uses the metaphor of dynamite to illustrate how the combination of the perfect gospel of Jesus Christ with a divinely commissioned church can be transformative.
   
2. **Power of Covenants and Ordinances**: The Church provides a framework to teach the gospel and administer sacred ordinances under God's authority which draws individuals closer to God and allows them to access divine power.
   
3. **Transformation Through Christ**: By remaining faithful to the covenants made within the Church, members can become transformed and draw closer to becoming what God intends them to be.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- **3 Nephi 27:21**: Importance of following the Savior's example.
- **Mosiah 26:22, 27:13**: Emphasizing faith and repentance through the Church.
- **Alma 7:11-13**: Christ's atoning sacrifice and His role in our transformation.
- **Doctrine of Christ**: The essential components of faith, repentance, baptism, the Holy Ghost, and enduring to the end.

### Questions to Ponder

- How does the combination of the gospel and the Church lead to greater access to God's power in my life?
- In what ways can I commit more fully to the Savior, His gospel, and His Church?
- How can understanding the role of the Church in God’s plan enhance my spiritual growth and transformation?
