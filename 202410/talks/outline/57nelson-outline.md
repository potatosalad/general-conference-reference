### Summary

President Russell M. Nelson emphasized the importance of preparing for the Second Coming of Jesus Christ by making discipleship a priority and engaging in temple worship.

### Haiku

Temples rise in haste,  
Prepare for Christ's return soon;  
Peace amidst chaos.

### Key Points

1. **Temple Building and Worship**  
   - The Church is building temples at an unprecedented pace as directed by the Lord, to gather Israel and prepare for the Second Coming.
  
2. **Preparation for the Second Coming**  
   - Now is the time to prepare for Christ's return through sincere discipleship and making Christ the center of our lives despite worldly distractions.
  
3. **Power of the Atonement**  
   - Embracing the Savior's Atonement provides comfort, peace, and healing. Devoting time to understand His sacrifice will bless our lives.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- **Isaiah 40:5**: The glory of the Lord shall be revealed.
- **Philippians 4:7**: The peace of God, which passeth all understanding.
- **Doctrine and Covenants 84:88**: The Lord's promise to be with us and provide heavenly help.
- **Mosiah 4:2-3**: The experience of being "swallowed up in the joy of Christ."

### Questions to Ponder

- How can I make going to the temple a regular part of my worship to strengthen my relationship with Jesus Christ?
- What steps can I take to ensure that I am spiritually prepared for the Second Coming?
- How can a better understanding of the Atonement of Jesus Christ improve my life and help me cope with challenges?
- In what ways can I actively participate in the gathering of Israel as I prepare for the Savior's return?
