### Summary

President Emily Belle Freeman teaches that understanding and keeping priesthood ordinances and covenants allow us to access God's power, helping us become the individuals God envisions.

### Haiku

God's power flows in,  
Through covenant paths we take,  
Become who you're meant.

### Key Points

1. **Authority and Ordinances**: Ordinances must be performed with divine authority; this enables them to manifest God's power.
   
2. **Covenants and Promises**: It is not just making covenants but keeping them that unlocks the power of God in our lives.
   
3. **Personal Application**: Through prayerful study and accessing priesthood ordinances, individuals can experience inward sanctification, divine guidance, and become more like Christ.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- [Doctrine and Covenants 25](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/25?lang=eng) - Revelation to Emma Smith.
- Principles of priesthood and the example of priesthood restoration in Harmony.
- President Russell M. Nelson's teachings on accessing God's power through keeping covenants.

### Questions to Ponder

- How do priesthood ordinances and covenants enhance your spiritual strength and guidance from God?
- In what ways can you more fully keep the covenants you've made to experience God's power in your life?
- How can studying specific doctrines, like those given to Emma Smith, deepen your understanding of your divine potential and responsibilities?
