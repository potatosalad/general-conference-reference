### Key Principles

1. *Sustaining of General Authorities, Area Seventies, and General Officers - President Henry B. Eyring:* Members reaffirm their support for Church leaders, underscoring unity and collective recognition of divine leadership.

2. *The Triumph of Hope - Elder Neil L. Andersen:* Hope is a divine gift that grows with faith in Jesus Christ, sustaining us through trials.

3. *Live Up to Your Privileges - President Emily Belle Freeman:* Participation in priesthood ordinances and covenants connects us to divine power.

4. *God’s Favourite - Elder Karl D. Hirst:* Recognizing and accepting God's love enhances our resilience and joy.

5. *“This Is My Gospel”—“This Is My Church” - Elder Dale G. Renlund:* The gospel and Church of Jesus Christ offer transformative spiritual power.

6. *Trusting Our Father - Elder David P. Homer:* Trusting God in all aspects of life leads to peace and alignment with eternal truths.

7. *God Loves All His Children - Elder Gregorio E. Casillas:* We are called to serve others and bring them closer to Christ.

8. *Following Christ - President Dallin H. Oaks:* Following Christ's teachings fosters love and peacemaking.

9. *Burying Our Weapons of Rebellion - Elder D. Todd Christofferson:* Surrendering rebellion to God deepens our conversion and joy.

10. *Bonded to Jesus Christ: Becoming the Salt of the Earth - Elder José A. Teixeira:* Remaining connected to Christ helps us spiritually enrich our lives and others'.

11. *His Hand Ready to Help Us - Elder Juan Pablo Villar:* Christ is ever-present to help us when we reach out in faith.

12. *Welcome to the Church of Joy - Elder Patrick Kearon:* Embrace joy as central to gospel living and church worship.

13. *“Ye Are My Friends” - Elder David L. Buckner:* Building high and holy relationships mirrors the Savior's friendship.

14. *Be Thou Clean - Elder D. Martin Goury:* Daily repentance maintains spiritual cleanliness and receptivity to the Holy Ghost.

15. *The Wind Did Never Cease to Blow - Elder Aroldo B. Cavalcante:* Service and support can guide others to receive God's blessings.

16. *Aligning Our Will with His - Elder Ulisses Soares:* Aligning our will with God's helps us attain eternal treasures.

17. *Holiness to the Lord in Everyday Life - Elder Gerrit W. Gong:* Integrating holiness in daily activities brings joy and closeness to God.

18. *The Joy of Our Redemption - Sister Kristin M. Yee:* The Savior's atonement redeems us, transforming weaknesses into strength.

19. *The Man Who Communed with Jehovah - Elder Kyle S. McKay:* Joseph Smith's role in the Restoration is pivotal and invites continual revelation.

20. *Embrace the Lord’s Gift of Repentance - Elder Jorge M. Alvarado:* Continual repentance empowers immediate joy and spiritual growth.

21. *In the Space of Not Many Years - Elder David A. Bednar:* Pride can turn divine prosperity into a distraction; remain humble and faithful.

22. *“I Am He” - President Jeffrey R. Holland:* Christ's charity exemplifies divine obedience calling us to follow His teachings.

23. *Seeking Answers to Spiritual Questions - Sister Tracy Y. Browning:* Sincere questions lead to growth through faith-led learning.

24. *Mortality Works! - Elder Brook P. Hales:* Heavenly Father's plan through mortality is designed for our success.

25. *Seek Him with All Your Heart - Bishop L. Todd Budge:* Regular communion with God enriches worship and spiritual strength.

26. *Days Never to Be Forgotten - Elder Gary E. Stevenson:* Anticipate fulfilling opportunities to share the gospel through service.

27. *O Youth of the Noble Birthright - Brother Bradley R. Wilcox:* Youth are trusted with divine responsibilities in gathering Israel.

28. *Simple Is the Doctrine of Jesus Christ - President Henry B. Eyring:* Teaching and living the simple doctrines of Christ fortify spiritual safety.

29. *Nourish the Roots, and the Branches Will Grow - Elder Dieter F. Uchtdorf:* Rooting our faith deep in Christ naturally strengthens our testimonies.

30. *The Words of Christ and the Holy Ghost Will Lead Us to the Truth - Elder Takashi Wada:* Embrace the guidance of scriptures and the Holy Ghost to fulfill divine potential.

31. *“Behold I Am the Light Which Ye Shall Hold Up” - Elder Ronald A. Rasband:* Reflecting Christ through covenants and following the prophet holds up His light.

32. *Sacred Scriptures—the Foundations of Faith - Elder Quentin L. Cook:* Scriptures are crucial for conversion and faithfulness in the gospel.

33. *Sons and Daughters of God - Elder Rubén V. Alliaud:* We are literal children of God with the potential to become like Him.

34. *Focus on Jesus Christ and His Gospel - Elder I. Raymond Egbo:* Prioritizing Christ over distractions leads to true joy and success.

35. *The Lord Jesus Christ Will Come Again - President Russell M. Nelson:* Prepare for the Second Coming by prioritizing discipleship and temple worship.
