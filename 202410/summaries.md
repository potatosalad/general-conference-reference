1. Sustaining of General Authorities, Area Seventies, and General Officers - Presented by President Henry B. Eyring



President Henry B. Eyring presented and received the sustaining vote for General Authorities, Area Seventies, and General Officers of the Church, reaffirming the support and recognition of their roles by the members.

2. The Triumph of Hope - Elder Neil L. Andersen

Hope is a living gift, a gift that grows as we increase our faith in Jesus Christ.

Elder Neil L. Andersen explored the enduring gift of hope that grows as our faith in Jesus Christ increases, emphasizing how this divine hope can sustain us through life's trials and lead us towards eternal joy.

3. Live Up to Your Privileges - President Emily Belle Freeman

Learn how priesthood ordinances and covenant promises will allow God’s power to flow into your life.

President Emily Belle Freeman teaches that understanding and keeping priesthood ordinances and covenants allow us to access God's power, helping us become the individuals God envisions.

4. God’s Favourite - Elder Karl D. Hirst

Being filled with God’s love shields us in life’s storms but also makes the happy moments happier.

Elder Karl D. Hirst emphasizes that God's love is constant and limitless, urging individuals to recognize their own worthiness of divine love amidst life's challenges and joys.

5. “This Is My Gospel”—“This Is My Church” - Elder Dale G. Renlund

This is the Savior’s gospel, and this is His Church (see 3 Nephi 27:21; Mosiah 26:22; 27:13). The combination of the two is powerful and transformative.

Elder Dale G. Renlund explains that the combination of the Savior's gospel and His Church offers powerful and transformative blessings.

6. Trusting Our Father - Elder David P. Homer

God trusts us to make many important decisions, and in all matters He asks us to trust Him.

Elder David P. Homer emphasizes the importance of trusting God in all decisions and acknowledges that God’s eternal truths are absolute, reminding us to submit our will to His for ultimate growth and peace.

7. God Loves All His Children - Elder Gregorio E. Casillas

Jesus Christ is always calling upon us, and He uses us, His ordinary servants, to help bring His children to Him.

Elder Gregorio E. Casillas emphasizes that Jesus Christ constantly calls and uses us to assist in bringing His children closer to Him as we act as His disciples and minister to others.

8. Following Christ - President Dallin H. Oaks

As followers of Christ, we teach and testify of Jesus Christ, our Perfect Role Model. So let us follow Him by forgoing contention.

President Dallin H. Oaks encourages followers of Christ to embrace a lifestyle defined by continuous commitment to His teachings, emphasizing the importance of love, avoiding contention, and being peacemakers in all interactions.

9. Burying Our Weapons of Rebellion - Elder D. Todd Christofferson

May we bury—very, very deep—any element of rebellion against God in our lives and replace it with a willing heart and a willing mind.

Elder D. Todd Christofferson invites us to bury any forms of rebellion against God by cultivating willing hearts and minds, leading us to deeper conversion and joy.

10. Bonded to Jesus Christ: Becoming the Salt of the Earth - Elder José A. Teixeira

As we remain bonded to the Lord, our lives will naturally reflect His light, and we will become the salt of the earth.

Elder José A. Teixeira emphasizes the importance of being bonded to Jesus Christ to become the "salt of the earth," enriching and preserving our spiritual vitality through service, meaningful worship, and living Christlike lives.

11. His Hand Ready to Help Us - Elder Juan Pablo Villar

As we reach out to Jesus Christ in faith, He will always be there.

Elder Juan Pablo Villar emphasizes that as we reach out to Jesus Christ in faith, He will always be there to help us overcome challenges and adversities.

12. Welcome to the Church of Joy - Elder Patrick Kearon

Because of the redeeming life and mission of our Saviour, Jesus Christ, we can—and should—be the most joyful people on earth!

Elder Patrick Kearon invites us to embrace joy in our worship, especially during sacrament meetings, as we are members of the church of joy centered on Jesus Christ.

13. “Ye Are My Friends” - Elder David L. Buckner

The Savior’s declaration “ye are my friends” is a clarion call to build higher and holier relationships among all of God’s children.

In his talk "Ye Are My Friends," Elder David L. Buckner emphasizes that Jesus Christ calls us His friends and urges us to build higher and holier relationships with others to achieve unity and belonging among God's children.

14. Be Thou Clean - Elder D. Martin Goury

Daily repentance allows us to discern the guidance of the Lord through the Holy Ghost.

Elder D. Martin Goury emphasized the importance of daily repentance to remain spiritually clean and better receive the promptings of the Holy Ghost, thereby aiding us in our spiritual journey.

15. The Wind Did Never Cease to Blow - Elder Aroldo B. Cavalcante

We can help others progress in their journey to receive God’s blessings.

Elder Aroldo B. Cavalcante discusses the importance of being aware of and serving those who may feel forgotten, urging us to act as winds of support and guidance, similar to the winds that guided the Jaredites to the promised land.

16. Aligning Our Will with His - Elder Ulisses Soares

Following the Lord’s will in our life will enable us to find the most precious pearl in the world—the kingdom of heaven.

Elder Ulisses Soares emphasizes the importance of aligning our desires and will with God’s will to obtain the eternal treasures of His kingdom.

17. Holiness to the Lord in Everyday Life - Elder Gerrit W. Gong

Holiness to the Lord makes daily living sacred. It draws us closer and happier to the Lord and each other.

Elder Gerrit W. Gong emphasizes that making "Holiness to the Lord" a part of our everyday lives sanctifies our daily activities, brings us joy, and draws us closer to God and each other.

18. The Joy of Our Redemption - Sister Kristin M. Yee

Jesus Christ’s love and power can save each of us from our mistakes, weaknesses, and sins and help us to become something more.

Sister Kristin M. Yee emphasizes the Savior's power to redeem individuals from mistakes and weaknesses, guiding them toward greater potential through His love and atonement.

19. The Man Who Communed with Jehovah - Elder Kyle S. McKay

Joseph Smith was “blessed to open the last dispensation,” and we are blessed that he did.

Elder Kyle S. McKay's talk emphasizes the pivotal role of Joseph Smith in the Restoration, highlighting the power of prayer, repentance, and the continuous guidance from the Lord through personal and collective revelation.

20. Embrace the Lord’s Gift of Repentance - Elder Jorge M. Alvarado

Let us not wait for things to get hard before turning to God. Let us not wait until the end of our mortal lives to truly repent.

Elder Jorge M. Alvarado's talk emphasizes the vital importance of embracing the Lord's gift of repentance, encouraging an immediate and continuous turn to God, and highlighting that true joy comes through sincere repentance and faith in Jesus Christ.

21. In the Space of Not Many Years - Elder David A. Bednar

If we are not faithful and obedient, we can transform the God-given blessing of prosperity into a prideful curse that diverts and distracts us.

Elder Bednar discusses how pride can transform the blessing of prosperity into a curse, using the contrasting behaviors of the Nephites and Lamanites in the Book of Helaman as a warning for today.

22. “I Am He” - President Jeffrey R. Holland

Christ’s charity—evident in complete loyalty to divine will—persisted and continues to persist.

President Jeffrey R. Holland taught about Christ's unwavering charity, emphasizing that His obedience and love during His trials illustrate His divine mission and inspire us to follow His commandments more fully.

23. Seeking Answers to Spiritual Questions - Sister Tracy Y. Browning

Our sincere gospel questions can provide Heavenly Father and Jesus Christ with opportunities to help us grow.

Sister Tracy Y. Browning explains that our sincere gospel questions can provide opportunities for growth through obedience and trust in God.

24. Mortality Works! - Elder Brook P. Hales

Despite the challenges we all face, our loving Heavenly Father has designed the plan of happiness such that we are not destined to fail.

Elder Brook P. Hales contends that despite life's challenges, Heavenly Father's plan for us is designed for success and growth through mortality.

25. Seek Him with All Your Heart - Bishop L. Todd Budge

If Jesus Christ sought quiet time to commune with God and to be strengthened by Him, it would be wise for us to do the same.

Bishop L. Todd Budge encourages believers to regularly commune with God through quiet, intentional worship, akin to savoring each sacred opportunity in our spiritual lives.

26. Days Never to Be Forgotten - Elder Gary E. Stevenson

Upcoming moments will provide members of the Church everywhere with increased opportunities to share the glad tidings of the gospel of Jesus Christ.

Elder Gary E. Stevenson discusses the significance of past and future decades in the history of the Church, emphasizing upcoming opportunities for Latter-day Saints to share the gospel through unprecedented experiences and service.

27. O Youth of the Noble Birthright - Brother Bradley R. Wilcox

God trusts you, the children of the covenant, to help with His work of bringing all His children safely home to Him.

Brother Bradley R. Wilcox encourages the youth of the Church to embrace their birthright and responsibilities as covenant children of God, emphasizing their role in helping bring others to Christ with love and trust from Heavenly Father.

28. Simple Is the Doctrine of Jesus Christ - President Henry B. Eyring

I bear witness of the sacred work of teaching Heavenly Father’s children the simple doctrine of Jesus Christ.

President Henry B. Eyring emphasizes the power and simplicity of teaching the doctrine of Jesus Christ, emphasizing repentance, faith, baptism, the gift of the Holy Ghost, and enduring to the end as essential principles for spiritual safety and family strength.

29. Nourish the Roots, and the Branches Will Grow - Elder Dieter F. Uchtdorf

The branches of your testimony will draw strength from your deepening faith in Heavenly Father and His Beloved Son.

Elder Dieter F. Uchtdorf emphasizes the importance of nourishing the roots of our faith in Jesus Christ, asserting that strong testimonies will naturally develop and flourish when grounded in core gospel principles.

30. The Words of Christ and the Holy Ghost Will Lead Us to the Truth - Elder Takashi Wada

Knowing this incredible plan will help us know we are children of God and we can become like Him.

Elder Takashi Wada teaches that by feasting on the words of Christ and listening to the guidance of the Holy Ghost, we can find direction and purpose in life, recognizing that we are children of God with divine potential.

31. “Behold I Am the Light Which Ye Shall Hold Up” - Elder Ronald A. Rasband

We hold up the Lord’s light when we hold fast to our covenants and when we support our living prophet.

Elder Ronald A. Rasband emphasizes the importance of holding up the light of Jesus Christ by adhering to covenants and supporting the living prophet.

32. Sacred Scriptures—the Foundations of Faith - Elder Quentin L. Cook

We cannot underestimate the significance of sacred scriptures both in conversions and in remaining faithful in the gospel.

Elder Quentin L. Cook emphasizes the crucial role of sacred scriptures, such as the Bible and the Book of Mormon, in guiding individuals to conversion and steadfastness in the gospel of Jesus Christ.

33. Sons and Daughters of God - Elder Rubén V. Alliaud

We truly believe that we are all literally the children of God, and because of that, we have the potential to become like Him.

Elder Rubén V. Alliaud emphasizes the powerful and joyful doctrine that we are literally children of God with the potential to become like Him, a belief fundamental to the teachings of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.

34. Focus on Jesus Christ and His Gospel - Elder I. Raymond Egbo

When we ignore the distractions of the world and focus on Christ and His gospel, we are guaranteed success.

Elder I. Raymond Egbo emphasizes that by focusing on Christ and His gospel, we can overcome life's distractions and experience meaningful joy and success.

35. The Lord Jesus Christ Will Come Again - President Russell M. Nelson

Now is the time for you and for me to prepare for the Second Coming of our Lord and Savior, Jesus the Christ.

President Russell M. Nelson emphasized the importance of preparing for the Second Coming of Jesus Christ by making discipleship a priority and engaging in temple worship.
