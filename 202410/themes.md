### Summary

The overarching theme of the latest General Conference was a profound emphasis on faith in Jesus Christ and the transformative power of His gospel. Many talks highlighted the importance of personal conversion, deepening one's relationship with the Savior, and the joy found in His Atonement. Repeated doctrines included the gift of repentance, the power of priesthood ordinances and covenants, and the vital role of maintaining hope and focus on Christ despite life's challenges. Additionally, several talks stressed the necessity of spiritual resilience and the collective effort to be peacemakers in an often contentious world. The message of personal preparation for the Second Coming of Jesus Christ was emphasized prominently.

### Questions to Ponder

- How does hope in Jesus Christ sustain you through trials? (See Elder Neil L. Andersen's talk on hope)
- What roles do priesthood ordinances and covenants play in accessing God's power in your life? (President Emily Belle Freeman)
- How can we more fully align our wills with God's will to find true joy? (Elder Ulisses Soares)
- Consider the power of repentance and its role in drawing us closer to Jesus Christ. (See Elder Jorge M. Alvarado's talk)
- How can focusing on scriptural study anchor us in faith amid life's distractions? (Elder Quentin L. Cook)
