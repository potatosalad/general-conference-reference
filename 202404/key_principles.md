### Key Principles

1. *Sustaining of General Authorities, Area Seventies, and General Officers - Presented by President Dallin H. Oaks:* Reaffirming common consent under priesthood keys unites the Church in Christ-centered, covenant governance.
2. *Church Auditing Department Report, 2023 - Presented by Jared B. Larson:* Faithful stewardship of sacred funds reflects integrity, provident living, and trust in God’s revealed order.
3. *Motions of a Hidden Fire - President Jeffrey R. Holland:* Prayerful discipleship and consecrated endurance anchor us in Christ’s timing and mercy.
4. *Put Ye On the Lord Jesus Christ - Sister J. Anette Dennis:* Covenants help us “put on” Christ, inviting His protection, guidance, and transforming grace.
5. *Pillars and Rays - Elder Alexander Dushku:* Testimony grows “one ray at a time” through consistent revelation that brightens faith in Jesus Christ.
6. *Covenant Confidence through Jesus Christ - Elder Ulisses Soares:* Temple covenants provide divine assurance and power to become holier disciples amid modern challenges.
7. *Integrity: A Christlike Attribute - Elder Jack N. Gerard:* Christlike integrity—true to God, others, and self—fortifies covenant living in a turbulent world.
8. *All Will Be Well Because of Temple Covenants - President Henry B. Eyring:* Keeping temple covenants brings the Savior’s peace, revelation, and enduring family bonds.
9. *“Be Still, and Know That I Am God” - Elder David A. Bednar:* Holy times and places—Sabbath, temple, and home—help us be still, hear the Spirit, and stand firm in Christ.
10. *Rise! He Calleth Thee - Elder Massimo De Feo:* Turning to Christ with real intent clarifies our vision and strengthens us to face trials faithfully.
11. *A Record of What I Have Both Seen and Heard - Elder Brent H. Nielson:* The accelerating Restoration—temples, missions, and covenants—makes now a blessed time to follow Christ.
12. *Jesus Christ at the Center of Our Lives - Elder Jose L. Alonso:* Centering life in Jesus Christ brings healing, revelation, and durable joy through His Atonement.
13. *All Things for Our Good - Elder Gerrit W. Gong:* In God’s eternal plan, covenants and Christ’s Atonement consecrate all things for our ultimate good.
14. *In Support of the Rising Generation - Brother Michael T. Nelson:* Loving relationships and united councils help youth learn revelation and remain on the covenant path.
15. *Be One with Christ - Elder Quentin L. Cook:* True belonging comes as we become one with Christ, keep covenants, and include all of God’s children.
16. *Miracles, Angels, and Priesthood Power - Elder Shayne M. Bowen:* Priesthood keys and faithful covenant living open the way for miracles and angelic ministration.
17. *Foreordained to Serve - Elder Steven R. Bangerter:* As we seek revelation, God unveils our foreordained purpose in gathering Israel and serving Him now.
18. *Faithful to the End - Sister Andrea Muñoz Spannaus:* Youth conquer “Goliaths” through love of God, daily repentance, covenants, and a personal testimony of Christ.
19. *Fruit That Remains - Elder Matthew L. Carpenter:* The Holy Spirit of Promise seals faithful ordinances—especially marriage—so blessings endure eternally.
20. *A Higher Joy - Elder Dieter F. Uchtdorf:* Higher joy is found in following Jesus—drawing near to God, seeking joy, and lifting others.
21. *Words Matter - Elder Ronald A. Rasband:* Feasting on God’s words and speaking by the Spirit sustains prophets and builds Christlike unity.
22. *Pray, He Is There - President Susan H. Porter:* Prayer helps us know God, become like the Savior, and show His love in His timing.
23. *The Powerful, Virtuous Cycle of the Doctrine of Christ - Elder Dale G. Renlund:* Intentional, repeated living of the doctrine of Christ creates covenant momentum and ministering power.
24. *Trust in the Lord - Elder Paul B. Pieper:* Continuous, wholehearted trust in God deepens our relationship with Him and directs faithful action.
25. *God’s Intent Is to Bring You Home - Elder Patrick Kearon:* The Father’s plan and Christ’s Atonement remove barriers and invite everyone home through covenants.
26. *Swallowed Up in the Joy of Christ - Elder Brian K. Taylor:* As we “think celestial,” the Lord consecrates afflictions and fills us with His covenant joy.
27. *Covenants and Responsibilities - President Dallin H. Oaks:* Covenants—central to the Restoration—bind us to Jesus Christ and lead to salvation and exaltation.
28. *The Testimony of Jesus - Elder D. Todd Christofferson:* Be valiant in the testimony of Jesus by keeping covenants, heeding prophets, and pursuing holiness.
29. *Call, Don’t Fall - Elder Taylor G. Godoy:* In uncertainty, call on God first; act in faith and trust His timing, and you will stand.
30. *Bridging the Two Great Commandments - Elder Gary E. Stevenson:* Discipleship requires balanced devotion to loving God and loving our neighbor.
31. *Opposition in All Things - Elder Mathias Held:* Opposition enables agency and growth, and Christ’s Atonement turns trials into spiritual gain.
32. *Temples, Houses of the Lord Dotting the Earth - Elder Neil L. Andersen:* Worthy temple worship arms covenant disciples with power, protection, and the Holy Ghost.
33. *It Is Wisdom in the Lord That We Should Have the Book of Mormon - President Mark L. Pace:* Daily Book of Mormon study invites the Spirit and safeguards against personal apostasy.
34. *Rejoice in the Gift of Priesthood Keys - President Russell M. Nelson:* Regular temple worship under restored priesthood keys unlocks gathering, sealing, and revelatory power in Christ.
