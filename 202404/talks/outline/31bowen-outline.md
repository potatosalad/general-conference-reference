### Summary

Elder Shayne M. Bowen testifies that priesthood keys have been restored in the latter days and, as we walk the covenant path and act in faith, we can experience miracles, the ministry of angels, and the guiding power of the Holy Ghost according to God’s will and timing.

### Haiku

Heavens are open—  
keys unlock covenant paths;  
angels bear us up.

### Key Points

1) Priesthood keys and living prophets  
   - Ancient priesthood keys were restored through Joseph Smith by Peter, James, and John; today President Russell M. Nelson holds and exercises all priesthood keys to direct the Savior’s Church.

2) Covenants and priesthood power bring God’s help  
   - Through ordinances and covenants (baptism, confirmation, temple), patriarchal blessings, and priesthood blessings, God grants comfort, healing, guidance, and power; miracles occur—sometimes immediately, sometimes unfolding over time—but God always keeps His promises.

3) Seek and expect miracles; angels minister  
   - As we participate in priesthood work and faithfully walk the covenant path, we can seek and expect miracles and the ministry of angels; outcomes come in the Lord’s timing and according to His plan.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- Matthew 16:19; John 21:15–17 — The Savior entrusts keys and leadership to His Apostles.  
- Doctrine and Covenants 27:12–13 — Restoration of priesthood keys and “a dispensation of the gospel.”  
- Doctrine and Covenants 84:20–21 — “In the ordinances thereof, the power of godliness is manifest.”  
- Doctrine and Covenants 84:88 — The Lord’s promise of angels “round about you, to bear you up.”  
- Doctrine and Covenants 107:3–4 — “The Holy Priesthood, after the Order of the Son of God.”  
- Moroni 7:27–29, 35–37 — Miracles and angels have not ceased.  
- 2 Nephi 32:3 — Angels speak by the power of the Holy Ghost (words of Christ).  
- President Russell M. Nelson, “The Power of Spiritual Momentum” (Apr. 2022) — Seek and expect miracles.  
- Gospel principles: priesthood keys and authority; covenant path and temple covenants; patriarchal blessings; priesthood blessings of comfort and healing; submission to God’s will and timing; ministry of angels.

- How do priesthood keys operate through living prophets to bless my life today?  
- In what specific ways do my covenants connect me to God’s power each day?  
- When have I seen miracles come immediately, and when have I seen them unfold over time?  
- How can my patriarchal blessing anchor my faith during fear, uncertainty, or trials?  
- What does it mean to “seek and expect miracles” while fully trusting God’s timing and will?  
- How can I better prepare to give or receive priesthood blessings with faith and meekness?  
- In what ways can women and men, youth and adults, participate in priesthood work together?  
- What is my next step on the covenant path that will invite more of heaven’s help?
