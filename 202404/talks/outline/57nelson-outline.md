### Summary

President Nelson rejoices in the Restoration of priesthood keys—especially those restored in the Kirtland Temple—and promises that faithful, regular temple worship unlocks gathering, Abrahamic, and sealing blessings, bringing divine power, protection, revelation, and joy to every covenant-keeping disciple.

### Haiku

Priesthood keys restored  
Open covenants, bind hearts;  
Temples light our way.

### Key Points

1. Restoration and meaning of priesthood keys  
   - In the Kirtland Temple, the Savior, Moses, Elias, and Elijah restored essential keys: gathering Israel, the gospel of Abraham, and the sealing power. These keys distinguish the Lord’s Church and enable ordinances and covenants that bless us in mortality and beyond.

2. Temples as the channel of power and protection  
   - The Kirtland dedicatory prayer (Doctrine and Covenants 109) teaches that the temple is a house of prayer, fasting, faith, learning, glory, order, and of God—promising answers, revelation, a fulness of the Holy Ghost, angelic help, courage, and that “no combination of wickedness” will prevail against the faithful.

3. God’s love extended to all through gathering, Abrahamic blessings, and sealing  
   - The gathering of Israel, the gospel of Abraham, and the sealing power demonstrate God’s universal love; through temple worship we help gather Israel on both sides of the veil and receive every blessing promised to Abraham, as the Church increases temple access worldwide.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- Doctrine and Covenants 110:1–16 — Christ, Moses, Elias, and Elijah appear in the Kirtland Temple to confer keys.  
- Doctrine and Covenants 109 — Kirtland Temple dedicatory prayer; promises of revelation, power, protection, and a fulness of the Holy Ghost.  
- Malachi 4:5–6; Doctrine and Covenants 2 — Elijah’s promise to turn hearts of fathers and children; sealing power.  
- 2 Nephi 26:33 — “All are alike unto God”; God’s universal invitation.  
- 1 Nephi 8; 1 Nephi 11–15 — Holding fast to the iron rod amid mists of darkness.  
- Abraham 2:9–11 — The Abrahamic covenant: blessings to all nations.  
- Doctrine and Covenants 128:18 — The welding link of sealing across the veil.  
- Gospel principles: Priesthood keys and authority, the gathering of Israel, the Abrahamic covenant, the sealing power, temple worship as a source of personal revelation and divine protection, “think celestial” living.
