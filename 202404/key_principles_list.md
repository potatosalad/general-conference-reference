### Key Principles

1. Reaffirming common consent under priesthood keys unites the Church in Christ-centered, covenant governance. ([&ldquo;Sustaining of General Authorities, Area Seventies, and General Officers&rdquo; - Presented by President Dallin H. Oaks](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/11oaks?lang=eng))
2. Faithful stewardship of sacred funds reflects integrity, provident living, and trust in God’s revealed order. ([&ldquo;Church Auditing Department Report, 2023&rdquo; - Presented by Jared B. Larson](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/12larson?lang=eng))
3. Prayerful discipleship and consecrated endurance anchor us in Christ’s timing and mercy. ([&ldquo;Motions of a Hidden Fire&rdquo; - President Jeffrey R. Holland](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/13holland?lang=eng))
4. Covenants help us “put on” Christ, inviting His protection, guidance, and transforming grace. ([&ldquo;Put Ye On the Lord Jesus Christ&rdquo; - Sister J. Anette Dennis](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/14dennis?lang=eng))
5. Testimony grows “one ray at a time” through consistent revelation that brightens faith in Jesus Christ. ([&ldquo;Pillars and Rays&rdquo; - Elder Alexander Dushku](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/15dushku?lang=eng))
6. Temple covenants provide divine assurance and power to become holier disciples amid modern challenges. ([&ldquo;Covenant Confidence through Jesus Christ&rdquo; - Elder Ulisses Soares](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/16soares?lang=eng))
7. Christlike integrity—true to God, others, and self—fortifies covenant living in a turbulent world. ([&ldquo;Integrity: A Christlike Attribute&rdquo; - Elder Jack N. Gerard](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/17gerard?lang=eng))
8. Keeping temple covenants brings the Savior’s peace, revelation, and enduring family bonds. ([&ldquo;All Will Be Well Because of Temple Covenants&rdquo; - President Henry B. Eyring](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/18eyring?lang=eng))
9. Holy times and places—Sabbath, temple, and home—help us be still, hear the Spirit, and stand firm in Christ. ([&ldquo;“Be Still, and Know That I Am God”&rdquo; - Elder David A. Bednar](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/21bednar?lang=eng))
10. Turning to Christ with real intent clarifies our vision and strengthens us to face trials faithfully. ([&ldquo;Rise! He Calleth Thee&rdquo; - Elder Massimo De Feo](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/22de-feo?lang=eng))
11. The accelerating Restoration—temples, missions, and covenants—makes now a blessed time to follow Christ. ([&ldquo;A Record of What I Have Both Seen and Heard&rdquo; - Elder Brent H. Nielson](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/23nielson?lang=eng))
12. Centering life in Jesus Christ brings healing, revelation, and durable joy through His Atonement. ([&ldquo;Jesus Christ at the Center of Our Lives&rdquo; - Elder Jose L. Alonso](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/24alonso?lang=eng))
13. In God’s eternal plan, covenants and Christ’s Atonement consecrate all things for our ultimate good. ([&ldquo;All Things for Our Good&rdquo; - Elder Gerrit W. Gong](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/25gong?lang=eng))
14. Loving relationships and united councils help youth learn revelation and remain on the covenant path. ([&ldquo;In Support of the Rising Generation&rdquo; - Brother Michael T. Nelson](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/26nelson?lang=eng))
15. True belonging comes as we become one with Christ, keep covenants, and include all of God’s children. ([&ldquo;Be One with Christ&rdquo; - Elder Quentin L. Cook](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/27cook?lang=eng))
16. Priesthood keys and faithful covenant living open the way for miracles and angelic ministration. ([&ldquo;Miracles, Angels, and Priesthood Power&rdquo; - Elder Shayne M. Bowen](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/31bowen?lang=eng))
17. As we seek revelation, God unveils our foreordained purpose in gathering Israel and serving Him now. ([&ldquo;Foreordained to Serve&rdquo; - Elder Steven R. Bangerter](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/32bangerter?lang=eng))
18. Youth conquer “Goliaths” through love of God, daily repentance, covenants, and a personal testimony of Christ. ([&ldquo;Faithful to the End&rdquo; - Sister Andrea Muñoz Spannaus](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/33spannaus?lang=eng))
19. The Holy Spirit of Promise seals faithful ordinances—especially marriage—so blessings endure eternally. ([&ldquo;Fruit That Remains&rdquo; - Elder Matthew L. Carpenter](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/34carpenter?lang=eng))
20. Higher joy is found in following Jesus—drawing near to God, seeking joy, and lifting others. ([&ldquo;A Higher Joy&rdquo; - Elder Dieter F. Uchtdorf](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/35uchtdorf?lang=eng))
21. Feasting on God’s words and speaking by the Spirit sustains prophets and builds Christlike unity. ([&ldquo;Words Matter&rdquo; - Elder Ronald A. Rasband](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/41rasband?lang=eng))
22. Prayer helps us know God, become like the Savior, and show His love in His timing. ([&ldquo;Pray, He Is There&rdquo; - President Susan H. Porter](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/42porter?lang=eng))
23. Intentional, repeated living of the doctrine of Christ creates covenant momentum and ministering power. ([&ldquo;The Powerful, Virtuous Cycle of the Doctrine of Christ&rdquo; - Elder Dale G. Renlund](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/43renlund?lang=eng))
24. Continuous, wholehearted trust in God deepens our relationship with Him and directs faithful action. ([&ldquo;Trust in the Lord&rdquo; - Elder Paul B. Pieper](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/44pieper?lang=eng))
25. The Father’s plan and Christ’s Atonement remove barriers and invite everyone home through covenants. ([&ldquo;God’s Intent Is to Bring You Home&rdquo; - Elder Patrick Kearon](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/45kearon?lang=eng))
26. As we “think celestial,” the Lord consecrates afflictions and fills us with His covenant joy. ([&ldquo;Swallowed Up in the Joy of Christ&rdquo; - Elder Brian K. Taylor](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/46taylor?lang=eng))
27. Covenants—central to the Restoration—bind us to Jesus Christ and lead to salvation and exaltation. ([&ldquo;Covenants and Responsibilities&rdquo; - President Dallin H. Oaks](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/47oaks?lang=eng))
28. Be valiant in the testimony of Jesus by keeping covenants, heeding prophets, and pursuing holiness. ([&ldquo;The Testimony of Jesus&rdquo; - Elder D. Todd Christofferson](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/51christofferson?lang=eng))
29. In uncertainty, call on God first; act in faith and trust His timing, and you will stand. ([&ldquo;Call, Don’t Fall&rdquo; - Elder Taylor G. Godoy](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/52godoy?lang=eng))
30. Discipleship requires balanced devotion to loving God and loving our neighbor. ([&ldquo;Bridging the Two Great Commandments&rdquo; - Elder Gary E. Stevenson](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/53stevenson?lang=eng))
31. Opposition enables agency and growth, and Christ’s Atonement turns trials into spiritual gain. ([&ldquo;Opposition in All Things&rdquo; - Elder Mathias Held](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/54held?lang=eng))
32. Worthy temple worship arms covenant disciples with power, protection, and the Holy Ghost. ([&ldquo;Temples, Houses of the Lord Dotting the Earth&rdquo; - Elder Neil L. Andersen](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/55andersen?lang=eng))
33. Daily Book of Mormon study invites the Spirit and safeguards against personal apostasy. ([&ldquo;It Is Wisdom in the Lord That We Should Have the Book of Mormon&rdquo; - President Mark L. Pace](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/56pace?lang=eng))
34. Regular temple worship under restored priesthood keys unlocks gathering, sealing, and revelatory power in Christ. ([&ldquo;Rejoice in the Gift of Priesthood Keys&rdquo; - President Russell M. Nelson](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2024/04/57nelson?lang=eng))
