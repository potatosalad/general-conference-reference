### Summary

Sister Andrea Muñoz Spannaus invites youth (and parents) to gain a personal, Spirit-confirmed testimony of living prophets and to heed their unified counsel—like the woman of Shunem welcomed Elisha—so we can hold fast to every good thing as we prepare for the Savior’s Second Coming.

### Haiku

Prophets speak today—  
Open hearts, build them a room;  
Hold fast to all good.

### Key Points

1. Recognize and receive God’s prophets by the Spirit  
   - Like the Shunammite woman discerned Elisha, we can seek and receive a personal witness and “make room” in our hearts and minds for prophetic counsel.

2. What prophets do and how they lead  
   - Prophets are seers and revelators who testify of Jesus Christ, teach truth, and prepare us for coming challenges; the First Presidency and Quorum of the Twelve act in unity to declare doctrine and guide the Church.

3. Act in faith at home and personally  
   - Parents are commanded to “teach freely,” raising children in light and truth; youth are invited to pray for a confirming witness and then obey with courage—thus laying hold upon every good thing as we move toward the Lord’s return.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- 2 Kings 4:8–10 — The Shunammite woman welcomes and makes room for the prophet Elisha.  
- Articles of Faith 1:9 — Continuing revelation in the latter days.  
- Doctrine and Covenants 1:38 — The Lord’s voice is heard through His servants.  
- Doctrine and Covenants 107:27 — Unanimity among the First Presidency and Twelve.  
- 3 Nephi 12:1 — Blessed are those who give heed to the Twelve.  
- Mosiah 15:1–2; Helaman 14:12; Ether 12:39 — Prophetic witnesses of Jesus Christ.  
- Moses 6:58; Doctrine and Covenants 93:40 — Parents teach “freely,” bringing children up in light and truth.  
- Moroni 7:19 — Lay hold upon every good thing.  
- Elder Gary E. Stevenson (General Conference, cited) — The Lord reveals His will to prophets to lead us to happiness and celestial glory.

### Questions to Ponder

- How can I “prepare a room” in my heart this week for the prophet’s counsel?  
- What recent invitation from the prophet or apostles am I acting on, and what is my next step?  
- When have I felt the Holy Ghost confirm that the First Presidency and Twelve are God’s anointed?  
- For parents: What does “teach freely” look like in our home—both formally and in daily moments?  
- Among the many voices today, how will I choose to “give heed” to living prophets?  
- Which “good things” do I need to hold fast to as I prepare for the Savior’s Second Coming?  
- How can our family create more “light and truth” in our media, routines, and conversations?  
- What blessings have I seen as I follow prophetic counsel with trust, faith, and hope?
