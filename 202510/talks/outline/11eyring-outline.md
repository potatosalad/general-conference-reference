### Summary

Elder Henry B. Eyring presented the sustaining of General Authorities, Area Seventies, and General Officers by common consent, affirming the Apostles as prophets, seers, and revelators and noting newly sustained Area Seventies.

### Haiku

Hands raised in consent,  
Hearts knit to serve the Savior—  
We sustain and act.

### Key Points

1) Common consent in the Church  
Members were invited to sustain Church leaders “in the usual way,” with any opposition to be shared through proper priesthood channels (stake president), reflecting order, unity, and accountability in the Lord’s Church.

2) Sustaining prophetic leadership  
The President of the Quorum of the Twelve and the Twelve were presented for sustaining as prophets, seers, and revelators—signifying our covenant willingness to support, follow, and pray for those called by God.

3) Organizational updates and continuity  
Two new Area Seventies—Bhanu K. Hiranandani and Fernando Vivas—were sustained, and the body of General Authorities, Area Seventies, and General Officers was sustained as presently constituted, affirming continuity in Church leadership.
