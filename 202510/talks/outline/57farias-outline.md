### Summary

Elder Ozani Farias testifies that the Book of Mormon is an immeasurable treasure that deepens our conversion to Jesus Christ as we study it diligently, feast upon its words, and bear testimony by sharing it with others.

### Haiku

Feast on sacred words  
Light grows with each page we turn—  
Christ’s voice shows the way.

### Key Points

1. Be diligent and consistent in daily study: Prioritize daily time in the Book of Mormon to gain spiritual strength, increased faith, and confidence in the Lord’s promises.
2. Feast upon the words of Christ: Move beyond reading to savoring, pondering, and applying; use study tools, record impressions, and “liken” the scriptures to your life to invite revelation.
3. Bear testimony and share the book: As your witness grows, share it—your testimony and even a marked copy can bless others and invite miracles through the power of Christ’s words.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- Moroni 10:4–5 — Ask in faith; the Holy Ghost manifests truth.
- Alma 17:2 — Diligent scripture study strengthens disciples.
- 2 Nephi 32:3 — Feast upon the words of Christ for direction.
- 1 Nephi 19:23 — Liken the scriptures to ourselves.
- 1 Nephi 8:12 — Desire to share the “fruit” (joy of the gospel) with others.
- Doctrine and Covenants 29:7 — The elect hear the Lord’s voice.
- Introduction to the Book of Mormon — Testament of Christ; plan of salvation; peace now and eternal life to come.
- The resurrected Savior’s ministry in 3 Nephi — The Book of Mormon centers on Jesus Christ and His doctrine.

### Questions to Ponder

- What specific time and place will I reserve each day to study the Book of Mormon?
- How can I turn my reading into “feasting” this week (tools, marking, journaling, questions to the Lord)?
- What “question of my soul” will I bring to my study, and how will I record the revelation I receive?
- Which verses should I liken to my current circumstances, and what action will I take because of them?
- Whom could I bless by sharing my testimony or a thoughtfully marked copy of the Book of Mormon?
- When have I felt Moroni’s promise fulfilled in my life, and how can I seek that witness again?
- How does focusing on Jesus Christ in the Book of Mormon help me make and keep sacred covenants right now?
