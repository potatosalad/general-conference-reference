### Key Principles

1. Sustain prophetic order and move forward in faith in Jesus Christ, honoring the prophet by advancing the Lord’s work with unity and reverence. ([&ldquo;Introduction&rdquo; - President Dallin H. Oaks](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/19oaks?lang=eng))
2. By common consent we sustain living prophets and apostles, strengthening covenant unity in Christ’s Church. ([&ldquo;Sustaining of General Authorities, Area Seventies, and General Officers&rdquo; - Presented by Elder Henry B. Eyring](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/11eyring?lang=eng))
3. Christlike peacemaking begins in the heart, heals homes, and builds covenant belonging in our communities and online. ([&ldquo;Blessed Are the Peacemakers&rdquo; - Elder Gary E. Stevenson](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/12stevenson?lang=eng))
4. Primary music teaches pure doctrine and anchors children to Jesus Christ and covenants for life. ([&ldquo;Tune Your Heart to Jesus Christ: The Sacred Gift of Primary Music&rdquo; - Sister Tracy Y. Browning](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/13browning?lang=eng))
5. The Lord transforms us as we turn our hearts fully to Him through sincere motives, discipleship, and covenants. ([&ldquo;The Lord Looketh on the Heart&rdquo; - Elder Ronald M. Barcellos](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/14barcellos?lang=eng))
6. Knowing we are beloved children of Heavenly Father changes how we repent, endure trials, and follow Christ. ([&ldquo;Know Who You Really Are&rdquo; - Elder Brik V. Eyre](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/15eyre?lang=eng))
7. Daily repentance, covenants, revelation, and heeding prophets reconcile us to God and produce unshakable faith in Christ. ([&ldquo;Be Reconciled to God&rdquo; - Elder Kelly R. Johnson](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/17johnson?lang=eng))
8. Discipleship is steady, disciplined effort while trusting Jesus Christ to perfect us one faithful step at a time. ([&ldquo;Do Your Part with All Your Heart&rdquo; - Elder Dieter F. Uchtdorf](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/16uchtdorf?lang=eng))
9. The Family Proclamation is revealed doctrine guiding equal partnership and peace on the covenant path in a family-centered gospel. ([&ldquo;The Family Proclamation—Words from God&rdquo; - Elder Ronald A. Rasband](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/21rasband?lang=eng))
10. Focusing on Christ and true doctrine invites the Holy Ghost to edify and deepen conversion for all learners. ([&ldquo;That All May Be Edified&rdquo; - Brother Chad H Webb](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/22webb?lang=eng))
11. Sacrament tables and temple altars bind us to Christ, granting mercy, protection, sanctification, healing, and rest. ([&ldquo;Humble Souls at Altars Kneel&rdquo; - Elder Jeremy R. Jaggi](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/23jaggi?lang=eng))
12. Testimony is a divine gift we must choose, nurture, and defend through the Holy Ghost and Christ-centered agency. ([&ldquo;The Eternal Gift of Testimony&rdquo; - Elder Kevin G. Brown](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/24brown?lang=eng))
13. The restored gospel creates a covenant culture of belonging where we gather, welcome, and minister so none are alone. ([&ldquo;No One Sits Alone&rdquo; - Elder Gerrit W. Gong](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/25gong?lang=eng))
14. Small and simple, covenant-centered acts keep our discipleship focused on Jesus Christ and bring daily joy. ([&ldquo;Simplicity in Christ&rdquo; - Elder Michael Cziesla](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/26cziesla?lang=eng))
15. As the Lord hastens His work, we lovingly welcome and nurture new and returning members on the temple-centered covenant path. ([&ldquo;The Lord Is Hastening His Work&rdquo; - Elder Quentin L. Cook](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/27cook?lang=eng))
16. Because of Christ’s Atonement, every soul can start again through repentance, covenants, and enabling grace. ([&ldquo;Jesus Christ and Your New Beginning&rdquo; - Elder Patrick Kearon](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/31kearon?lang=eng))
17. Since only the Savior fully knows our race, disciples show covenant love by cheering and lifting without judgment. ([&ldquo;Cheering Each Other On&rdquo; - Sister J. Anette Dennis](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/32dennis?lang=eng))
18. We show love for God by putting Him first, loving His children, being grateful, and choosing willing obedience. ([&ldquo;“Lovest Thou Me?”&rdquo; - Elder Steven C. Barlow](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/33barlow?lang=eng))
19. Christlike ministering counts and accounts by name so every soul is noticed, nourished, and led to Him. ([&ldquo;Remembering the Sheep&rdquo; - Elder William K. Jackson](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/34jackson?lang=eng))
20. Full forgiveness and deep healing come through the Savior’s atoning love as we exercise faith and repent. ([&ldquo;The Atoning Love of Jesus Christ&rdquo; - Elder Neil L. Andersen](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/35andersen?lang=eng))
21. God’s simple, sacred means—ordinances, priesthood, temples, and the Book of Mormon—manifest the Restoration and give spiritual sight in Christ. ([&ldquo;And Now I See&rdquo; - Elder Jeffrey R. Holland](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/41holland?lang=eng))
22. Christlike service, exemplified by service missionaries, opens hearts and invites all to minister regardless of circumstance. ([&ldquo;Go and Do Likewise&rdquo; - Elder James E. Evanson](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/42evanson?lang=eng))
23. Temperance, a Christlike self-mastery, fortifies our foundation in Christ and harmonizes all other virtues. ([&ldquo;Adorned with the Virtue of Temperance&rdquo; - Elder Ulisses Soares](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/43soares?lang=eng))
24. Ministering to the one with Christlike love invites souls to the temple and empowers true discipleship. ([&ldquo;The Power of Ministering to the One&rdquo; - Elder Peter M. Johnson](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/44johnson?lang=eng))
25. Individuals, families, and nations flourish by looking to God—making Christ, covenants, and prophetic counsel our highest priority. ([&ldquo;Look to God and Live&rdquo; - Elder D. Todd Christofferson](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/45christofferson?lang=eng))
26. Gaining and heeding a personal testimony of living prophets helps us hold fast to every good thing before the Second Coming. ([&ldquo;Prophets of God&rdquo; - Sister Andrea Muñoz Spannaus](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/46spannaus?lang=eng))
27. Trials are loving proving moments that refine us through faith in Christ to bear eternal glory. ([&ldquo;Proved and Strengthened in Christ&rdquo; - Elder Henry B. Eyring](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/47eyring?lang=eng))
28. Through agency, covenants, repentance, and godly fear, the Final Judgment becomes a pleasing acknowledgment of who we have become in Christ. ([&ldquo;They Are Their Own Judges&rdquo; - Elder David A. Bednar](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/51bednar?lang=eng))
29. Taking Christ’s name centers our identity as His covenant witnesses who quietly do good across generations. ([&ldquo;The Name by Which Ye Are Called&rdquo; - Elder B. Corey Cuvelier](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/52cuvelier?lang=eng))
30. Turn from “lying vanities,” honor covenants, and receive Christ’s immediate, personal mercy and joy amid trials. ([&ldquo;Forsake Not Your Own Mercy&rdquo; - Elder Matthew S. Holland](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/53holland?lang=eng))
31. Centering on Christ brings grateful joy and succor even in hardship, as witnessed among Saints in Africa. ([&ldquo;Smiling Faces and Grateful Hearts&rdquo; - Elder Carlos A. Godoy](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/54godoy?lang=eng))
32. Identifying with, remembering, and becoming like Jesus Christ aligns us with His work and unlocks covenant power. ([&ldquo;Taking on the Name of Jesus Christ&rdquo; - Elder Dale G. Renlund](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/55renlund?lang=eng))
33. Add more of Jesus through scripture, charity, small and simple actions, and revelation for a happier life. ([&ldquo;The Good News Recipe&rdquo; - Elder John D. Amos](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/56amos?lang=eng))
34. Feasting on and sharing the Book of Mormon deepens conversion to Jesus Christ. ([&ldquo;The Book of Mormon—an Immeasurable Treasure on Our Journey&rdquo; - Elder Ozani Farias](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/57farias?lang=eng))
35. The gospel is family-centered and temple-anchored, prioritizing ordinances worldwide and strengthening marriages, children, and service at home. ([&ldquo;The Family-Centered Gospel of Jesus Christ&rdquo; - President Dallin H. Oaks](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org//study/general-conference/2025/10/58oaks?lang=eng))
