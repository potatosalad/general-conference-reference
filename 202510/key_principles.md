### Key Principles

1. *Introduction - President Dallin H. Oaks:* Sustain prophetic order and move forward in faith in Jesus Christ, honoring the prophet by advancing the Lord’s work with unity and reverence.
2. *Sustaining of General Authorities, Area Seventies, and General Officers - Elder Henry B. Eyring:* By common consent we sustain living prophets and apostles, strengthening covenant unity in Christ’s Church.
3. *Blessed Are the Peacemakers - Elder Gary E. Stevenson:* Christlike peacemaking begins in the heart, heals homes, and builds covenant belonging in our communities and online.
4. *Tune Your Heart to Jesus Christ: The Sacred Gift of Primary Music - Sister Tracy Y. Browning:* Primary music teaches pure doctrine and anchors children to Jesus Christ and covenants for life.
5. *The Lord Looketh on the Heart - Elder Ronald M. Barcellos:* The Lord transforms us as we turn our hearts fully to Him through sincere motives, discipleship, and covenants.
6. *Know Who You Really Are - Elder Brik V. Eyre:* Knowing we are beloved children of Heavenly Father changes how we repent, endure trials, and follow Christ.
7. *Be Reconciled to God - Elder Kelly R. Johnson:* Daily repentance, covenants, revelation, and heeding prophets reconcile us to God and produce unshakable faith in Christ.
8. *Do Your Part with All Your Heart - Elder Dieter F. Uchtdorf:* Discipleship is steady, disciplined effort while trusting Jesus Christ to perfect us one faithful step at a time.
9. *The Family Proclamation—Words from God - Elder Ronald A. Rasband:* The Family Proclamation is revealed doctrine guiding equal partnership and peace on the covenant path in a family-centered gospel.
10. *That All May Be Edified - Brother Chad H Webb:* Focusing on Christ and true doctrine invites the Holy Ghost to edify and deepen conversion for all learners.
11. *Humble Souls at Altars Kneel - Elder Jeremy R. Jaggi:* Sacrament tables and temple altars bind us to Christ, granting mercy, protection, sanctification, healing, and rest.
12. *The Eternal Gift of Testimony - Elder Kevin G. Brown:* Testimony is a divine gift we must choose, nurture, and defend through the Holy Ghost and Christ-centered agency.
13. *No One Sits Alone - Elder Gerrit W. Gong:* The restored gospel creates a covenant culture of belonging where we gather, welcome, and minister so none are alone.
14. *Simplicity in Christ - Elder Michael Cziesla:* Small and simple, covenant-centered acts keep our discipleship focused on Jesus Christ and bring daily joy.
15. *The Lord Is Hastening His Work - Elder Quentin L. Cook:* As the Lord hastens His work, we lovingly welcome and nurture new and returning members on the temple-centered covenant path.
16. *Jesus Christ and Your New Beginning - Elder Patrick Kearon:* Because of Christ’s Atonement, every soul can start again through repentance, covenants, and enabling grace.
17. *Cheering Each Other On - Sister J. Anette Dennis:* Since only the Savior fully knows our race, disciples show covenant love by cheering and lifting without judgment.
18. *“Lovest Thou Me?” - Elder Steven C. Barlow:* We show love for God by putting Him first, loving His children, being grateful, and choosing willing obedience.
19. *Remembering the Sheep - Elder William K. Jackson:* Christlike ministering counts and accounts by name so every soul is noticed, nourished, and led to Him.
20. *The Atoning Love of Jesus Christ - Elder Neil L. Andersen:* Full forgiveness and deep healing come through the Savior’s atoning love as we exercise faith and repent.
21. *And Now I See - Elder Jeffrey R. Holland:* God’s simple, sacred means—ordinances, priesthood, temples, and the Book of Mormon—manifest the Restoration and give spiritual sight in Christ.
22. *Go and Do Likewise - Elder James E. Evanson:* Christlike service, exemplified by service missionaries, opens hearts and invites all to minister regardless of circumstance.
23. *Adorned with the Virtue of Temperance - Elder Ulisses Soares:* Temperance, a Christlike self-mastery, fortifies our foundation in Christ and harmonizes all other virtues.
24. *The Power of Ministering to the One - Elder Peter M. Johnson:* Ministering to the one with Christlike love invites souls to the temple and empowers true discipleship.
25. *Look to God and Live - Elder D. Todd Christofferson:* Individuals, families, and nations flourish by looking to God—making Christ, covenants, and prophetic counsel our highest priority.
26. *Prophets of God - Sister Andrea Muñoz Spannaus:* Gaining and heeding a personal testimony of living prophets helps us hold fast to every good thing before the Second Coming.
27. *Proved and Strengthened in Christ - Elder Henry B. Eyring:* Trials are loving proving moments that refine us through faith in Christ to bear eternal glory.
28. *They Are Their Own Judges - Elder David A. Bednar:* Through agency, covenants, repentance, and godly fear, the Final Judgment becomes a pleasing acknowledgment of who we have become in Christ.
29. *The Name by Which Ye Are Called - Elder B. Corey Cuvelier:* Taking Christ’s name centers our identity as His covenant witnesses who quietly do good across generations.
30. *Forsake Not Your Own Mercy - Elder Matthew S. Holland:* Turn from “lying vanities,” honor covenants, and receive Christ’s immediate, personal mercy and joy amid trials.
31. *Smiling Faces and Grateful Hearts - Elder Carlos A. Godoy:* Centering on Christ brings grateful joy and succor even in hardship, as witnessed among Saints in Africa.
32. *Taking on the Name of Jesus Christ - Elder Dale G. Renlund:* Identifying with, remembering, and becoming like Jesus Christ aligns us with His work and unlocks covenant power.
33. *The Good News Recipe - Elder John D. Amos:* Add more of Jesus through scripture, charity, small and simple actions, and revelation for a happier life.
34. *The Book of Mormon—an Immeasurable Treasure on Our Journey - Elder Ozani Farias:* Feasting on and sharing the Book of Mormon deepens conversion to Jesus Christ.
35. *The Family-Centered Gospel of Jesus Christ - President Dallin H. Oaks:* The gospel is family-centered and temple-anchored, prioritizing ordinances worldwide and strengthening marriages, children, and service at home.
