### Key Principles

1. Solemnly sustaining Church leaders reaffirms faith in the resurrected Jesus Christ as the head of His Church. ([&ldquo;Introduction&rdquo; - President Dallin H. Oaks](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/11oaks?lang=eng))
2. Members unite in faith as they sustain the Lord’s chosen prophet, apostles, and Church leaders. ([&ldquo;Solemn Assembly&rdquo; - Presented by President D. Todd Christofferson](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/12christofferson?lang=eng))
3. Serving faithfully in Church callings joins us with the Savior in His work and deepens discipleship. ([&ldquo;About His Business&rdquo; - Elder Patrick Kearon](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/13kearon?lang=eng))
4. True ministering is a covenant way of becoming like Jesus Christ by loving and serving individuals as He does. ([&ldquo;Ministering—“That Ye Love One Another; as I Have Loved You”&rdquo; - Sister Kristin M. Yee](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/14yee?lang=eng))
5. Jesus Christ lovingly calls all who feel distant to come home through His grace and the covenant path. ([&ldquo;Come Home&rdquo; - Elder Clark G. Gilbert](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/15gilbert?lang=eng))
6. Enduring to the end is lifelong transformation through Christ’s grace, covenants, spiritual gifts, and charity. ([&ldquo;All Who Have Endured Valiantly&rdquo; - Elder David A. Bednar](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/16bednar?lang=eng))
7. Following living prophets helps us follow Jesus Christ and reflect His light in a changing world. ([&ldquo;Follow the Prophet; He Knows the Way&rdquo; - Elder Michael John U. Teh](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/17teh?lang=eng))
8. Paying tithes and offerings puts God first and increases spiritual power, direction, and devotion. ([&ldquo;Tithing—Putting God First&rdquo; - Elder Jorge T. Becerra](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/18becerra?lang=eng))
9. Continual heartfelt prayer invites the Savior’s peace and sustaining help in every circumstance. ([&ldquo;Prayers for Peace&rdquo; - President Henry B. Eyring](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/19eyring?lang=eng))
10. Sustaining Church leaders expresses covenant support, unity, and gratitude for those called to serve. ([&ldquo;Sustaining of General Authorities, Area Seventies, and General Officers&rdquo; - Presented by President D. Todd Christofferson](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/21christofferson?lang=eng))
11. The Church’s sacred funds are administered with accountability, approved policies, and sound financial principles. ([&ldquo;Church Auditing Department Report, 2025&rdquo; - Presented by Jared B. Larson](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/22larson?lang=eng))
12. Because every soul is precious, disciples minister with careful Christlike attention so none are forgotten or lost. ([&ldquo;Lost Luggage, Redeemed Souls&rdquo; - Elder Gary E. Stevenson](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/23stevenson?lang=eng))
13. Lifelong discipleship requires a personal testimony of Jesus Christ and steady daily progress on the covenant path. ([&ldquo;Christ—Author and Finisher of Our Faith&rdquo; - Elder Eduardo F. Ortega](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/24ortega?lang=eng))
14. Through faith, repentance, and yielding our sins, we come to know the Father through Jesus Christ. ([&ldquo;“I Will Give Away All My Sins to Know Thee”&rdquo; - Elder Wan-Liang Wu](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/25wu?lang=eng))
15. Jesus Christ is the source of relief, strength, forgiveness, and hope as we walk with Him daily. ([&ldquo;Jesus Christ Is Not Our Burden; He Is Our Relief&rdquo; - Brother David J. Wunderli](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/26wunderli?lang=eng))
16. Christlike love embraces all humanity while personally caring for each individual soul. ([&ldquo;Love All; Love Each&rdquo; - Elder Gérald Caussé](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/27causse?lang=eng))
17. Jesus Christ is the only covenant path home, and we remain safe by keeping covenants and following His prophet. ([&ldquo;Jesus Christ Is the Way&rdquo; - Elder Brian J. Holmes](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/28holmes?lang=eng))
18. Jesus Christ knows us personally and gives covenant power as we take His name upon ourselves. ([&ldquo;He Knows You by Name&rdquo; - Elder Clement M. Matswagothata](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/29matswagothata?lang=eng))
19. Spiritual survival depends on consciously abiding in Jesus Christ amid the distracting voices of the world. ([&ldquo;Jesus Christ—the True Vine&rdquo; - Elder Ulisses Soares](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/210soares?lang=eng))
20. A personal witness that Jesus Christ lives changes us and moves us to share that witness through discipleship. ([&ldquo;Encounter at the Empty Tomb&rdquo; - President Dieter F. Uchtdorf](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/41uchtdorf?lang=eng))
21. Because of Jesus Christ, divine promises sustain us through both best and worst days with hope for what lies ahead. ([&ldquo;Best Days and Worst Days&rdquo; - President Emily Belle Freeman](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/42freeman?lang=eng))
22. Reverently partaking of the sacrament helps us feel the Savior’s love and receive the Holy Ghost’s companionship. ([&ldquo;I Feel My Savior’s Love&rdquo; - Elder Pedro X. Larreal](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/43larreal?lang=eng))
23. We must choose Jesus Christ as our personal guide through life’s hidden spiritual dangers and trust His healing power. ([&ldquo;Choose Jesus Christ as Your Guide&rdquo; - Elder Edward B. Rowe](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/44rowe?lang=eng))
24. Jesus Christ’s Resurrection conquers physical death for all and gives hope of joyful eternal reunions. ([&ldquo;He Is Risen&rdquo; - Elder Ronald A. Rasband](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/45rasband?lang=eng))
25. Focusing on Jesus Christ and His Atonement brings clarity, joy, forgiveness, healing, and hope. ([&ldquo;Because of Jesus Christ&rdquo; - Elder Dale G. Renlund](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/46renlund?lang=eng))
26. Covenant faithfulness through Jesus Christ brings divine love, strength in sorrow, and lasting joy with God. ([&ldquo;The Joy of a Covenant Relationship with God&rdquo; - Elder Thierry K. Mutombo](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/47mutombo?lang=eng))
27. Those who obey the Savior’s voice and keep covenants become the Lord’s treasured people and receive His blessings. ([&ldquo;A Peculiar Treasure&rdquo; - Elder Alan R. Walker](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/48walker?lang=eng))
28. The Resurrection gives eternal perspective and calls disciples to love all people and choose peacemaking over contention. ([&ldquo;Alive in Christ&rdquo; - President Dallin H. Oaks](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/49oaks?lang=eng))
29. Preparing for the Savior’s return requires a mighty change of heart so our motives and character become Christlike. ([&ldquo;The Character of Christ&rdquo; - President D. Todd Christofferson](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/51christofferson?lang=eng))
30. Remembering Jesus Christ leads us to obedience, righteous agency, covenant faithfulness, and salvation. ([&ldquo;Remember, Remember&rdquo; - Elder Chi Hong (Sam) Wong](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/52wong?lang=eng))
31. We deepen our relationship with Jesus Christ as we glory in His redeeming power, mercy, healing, and covenant love. ([&ldquo;I Glory in My Jesus&rdquo; - Elder Aaron T. Hall](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/53hall?lang=eng))
32. Children can offer their gifts to God and help bring others to Christ through faith in His plan and Atonement. ([&ldquo;“Here Am I, Send Me”&rdquo; - President Susan H. Porter](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/54porter?lang=eng))
33. Eternal marriage is a sacred covenant journey of becoming more like the Savior through faith, sacrifice, and enduring love. ([&ldquo;Eternal Marriage Is an Eternal Journey&rdquo; - Elder Neil L. Andersen](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/55andersen?lang=eng))
34. Through Christ’s Atonement, Resurrection, and restored priesthood keys, covenants can unite families eternally and bring us to the Father. ([&ldquo;Keys, Covenants, and Easter&rdquo; - Elder Quentin L. Cook](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/56cook?lang=eng))
35. Families and loved ones come unto Christ together through patient faith, agency, temple covenants, and trust in God’s timing. ([&ldquo;Come unto Christ—Together&rdquo; - Elder Taniela B. Wakolo](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/57wakolo?lang=eng))
36. The resurrected Christ walks with us through scripture, sacrament, covenants, the Holy Ghost, and loving fellowship. ([&ldquo;’Tis Eastertide: No One Walks Alone&rdquo; - Elder Gerrit W. Gong](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/58gong?lang=eng))
37. Jesus Christ is the way to peace and eternal life as we study prophetic messages and act in faith. ([&ldquo;Closing Remarks&rdquo; - President Dallin H. Oaks](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2026/04/59oaks?lang=eng))
