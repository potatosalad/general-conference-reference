### Key Principles

1. *Introduction - President Dallin H. Oaks:* Solemnly sustaining Church leaders reaffirms faith in the resurrected Jesus Christ as the head of His Church.
2. *Solemn Assembly - Presented by President D. Todd Christofferson:* Members unite in faith as they sustain the Lord’s chosen prophet, apostles, and Church leaders.
3. *About His Business - Elder Patrick Kearon:* Serving faithfully in Church callings joins us with the Savior in His work and deepens discipleship.
4. *Ministering—“That Ye Love One Another; as I Have Loved You” - Sister Kristin M. Yee:* True ministering is a covenant way of becoming like Jesus Christ by loving and serving individuals as He does.
5. *Come Home - Elder Clark G. Gilbert:* Jesus Christ lovingly calls all who feel distant to come home through His grace and the covenant path.
6. *All Who Have Endured Valiantly - Elder David A. Bednar:* Enduring to the end is lifelong transformation through Christ’s grace, covenants, spiritual gifts, and charity.
7. *Follow the Prophet; He Knows the Way - Elder Michael John U. Teh:* Following living prophets helps us follow Jesus Christ and reflect His light in a changing world.
8. *Tithing—Putting God First - Elder Jorge T. Becerra:* Paying tithes and offerings puts God first and increases spiritual power, direction, and devotion.
9. *Prayers for Peace - President Henry B. Eyring:* Continual heartfelt prayer invites the Savior’s peace and sustaining help in every circumstance.
10. *Sustaining of General Authorities, Area Seventies, and General Officers - Presented by President D. Todd Christofferson:* Sustaining Church leaders expresses covenant support, unity, and gratitude for those called to serve.
11. *Church Auditing Department Report, 2025 - Presented by Jared B. Larson:* The Church’s sacred funds are administered with accountability, approved policies, and sound financial principles.
12. *Lost Luggage, Redeemed Souls - Elder Gary E. Stevenson:* Because every soul is precious, disciples minister with careful Christlike attention so none are forgotten or lost.
13. *Christ—Author and Finisher of Our Faith - Elder Eduardo F. Ortega:* Lifelong discipleship requires a personal testimony of Jesus Christ and steady daily progress on the covenant path.
14. *“I Will Give Away All My Sins to Know Thee” - Elder Wan-Liang Wu:* Through faith, repentance, and yielding our sins, we come to know the Father through Jesus Christ.
15. *Jesus Christ Is Not Our Burden; He Is Our Relief - Brother David J. Wunderli:* Jesus Christ is the source of relief, strength, forgiveness, and hope as we walk with Him daily.
16. *Love All; Love Each - Elder Gérald Caussé:* Christlike love embraces all humanity while personally caring for each individual soul.
17. *Jesus Christ Is the Way - Elder Brian J. Holmes:* Jesus Christ is the only covenant path home, and we remain safe by keeping covenants and following His prophet.
18. *He Knows You by Name - Elder Clement M. Matswagothata:* Jesus Christ knows us personally and gives covenant power as we take His name upon ourselves.
19. *Jesus Christ—the True Vine - Elder Ulisses Soares:* Spiritual survival depends on consciously abiding in Jesus Christ amid the distracting voices of the world.
20. *Encounter at the Empty Tomb - President Dieter F. Uchtdorf:* A personal witness that Jesus Christ lives changes us and moves us to share that witness through discipleship.
21. *Best Days and Worst Days - President Emily Belle Freeman:* Because of Jesus Christ, divine promises sustain us through both best and worst days with hope for what lies ahead.
22. *I Feel My Savior’s Love - Elder Pedro X. Larreal:* Reverently partaking of the sacrament helps us feel the Savior’s love and receive the Holy Ghost’s companionship.
23. *Choose Jesus Christ as Your Guide - Elder Edward B. Rowe:* We must choose Jesus Christ as our personal guide through life’s hidden spiritual dangers and trust His healing power.
24. *He Is Risen - Elder Ronald A. Rasband:* Jesus Christ’s Resurrection conquers physical death for all and gives hope of joyful eternal reunions.
25. *Because of Jesus Christ - Elder Dale G. Renlund:* Focusing on Jesus Christ and His Atonement brings clarity, joy, forgiveness, healing, and hope.
26. *The Joy of a Covenant Relationship with God - Elder Thierry K. Mutombo:* Covenant faithfulness through Jesus Christ brings divine love, strength in sorrow, and lasting joy with God.
27. *A Peculiar Treasure - Elder Alan R. Walker:* Those who obey the Savior’s voice and keep covenants become the Lord’s treasured people and receive His blessings.
28. *Alive in Christ - President Dallin H. Oaks:* The Resurrection gives eternal perspective and calls disciples to love all people and choose peacemaking over contention.
29. *The Character of Christ - President D. Todd Christofferson:* Preparing for the Savior’s return requires a mighty change of heart so our motives and character become Christlike.
30. *Remember, Remember - Elder Chi Hong (Sam) Wong:* Remembering Jesus Christ leads us to obedience, righteous agency, covenant faithfulness, and salvation.
31. *I Glory in My Jesus - Elder Aaron T. Hall:* We deepen our relationship with Jesus Christ as we glory in His redeeming power, mercy, healing, and covenant love.
32. *“Here Am I, Send Me” - President Susan H. Porter:* Children can offer their gifts to God and help bring others to Christ through faith in His plan and Atonement.
33. *Eternal Marriage Is an Eternal Journey - Elder Neil L. Andersen:* Eternal marriage is a sacred covenant journey of becoming more like the Savior through faith, sacrifice, and enduring love.
34. *Keys, Covenants, and Easter - Elder Quentin L. Cook:* Through Christ’s Atonement, Resurrection, and restored priesthood keys, covenants can unite families eternally and bring us to the Father.
35. *Come unto Christ—Together - Elder Taniela B. Wakolo:* Families and loved ones come unto Christ together through patient faith, agency, temple covenants, and trust in God’s timing.
36. *’Tis Eastertide: No One Walks Alone - Elder Gerrit W. Gong:* The resurrected Christ walks with us through scripture, sacrament, covenants, the Holy Ghost, and loving fellowship.
37. *Closing Remarks - President Dallin H. Oaks:* Jesus Christ is the way to peace and eternal life as we study prophetic messages and act in faith.
