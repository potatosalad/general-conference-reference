### Summary

Elder Aaron T. Hall invites us to deepen our personal relationship with Jesus Christ by glorying in His divine names, redeeming power, mercy, healing, and covenant love.

### Haiku

My Jesus draws near
Mercy, healing, covenant
Risen Lord, my peace

### Key Points

1. Nephi and Ammon show how personal conversion leads us to glory joyfully in Jesus Christ.
2. The many names of Jesus Christ reveal His roles as Redeemer, Good Shepherd, Healer, Advocate, Lawgiver, and Deliverer.
3. As we draw near to Christ through repentance, covenants, obedience, and trust, He grants peace, power, mercy, and healing.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- [2 Nephi 33:6](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/2-ne/33?lang=eng&id=p6#p6) — “I glory in my Jesus, for he hath redeemed my soul from hell.”
- [2 Nephi 4:20–35](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/2-ne/4?lang=eng&id=p20-p35#p20) — Nephi’s psalm of trust in the Lord through affliction and weakness.
- [Alma 26:16](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/alma/26?lang=eng&id=p16#p16) — Ammon’s rejoicing: “Who can glory too much in the Lord?”
- [3 Nephi 9:14](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/3-ne/9?lang=eng&id=p14#p14) — “Repent, and I will receive you.”
- [Doctrine and Covenants 6:36](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/6?lang=eng&id=p36#p36) — “Look unto me in every thought; doubt not, fear not.”
- [Doctrine and Covenants 88:63](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/88?lang=eng&id=p63#p63) — “Draw near unto me and I will draw near unto you.”
- Jesus Christ’s Atonement, Resurrection, repentance, covenant relationship, and divine names.

### Questions to Ponder

- Which name or title of Jesus Christ feels most personal to me right now, and why?
- How have I seen the Savior act as my Redeemer, Healer, Advocate, or Deliverer?
- What can I do to make the phrase “my Jesus” more meaningful in my life?
- How can remembering Christ’s names help me find peace when life feels unfair or difficult?
- What covenant blessings help me receive greater access to the Savior’s power, love, and mercy?
