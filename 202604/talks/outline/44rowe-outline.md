### Summary

Elder Rowe teaches that because life contains unseen spiritual dangers, we must choose Jesus Christ as our perfect, personal guide and trust His power to heal us when we stumble.

### Haiku

Footsteps through danger
Christ leads, heals, and calls us home
Mercy lights the path

### Key Points

1. Jesus Christ invites all people to follow Him and no one needs to walk life’s path alone.
2. We stay close to Christ as our guide by walking the covenant path through faith, repentance, covenants, sacrament, scripture study, prayer, temple worship, and ministering.
3. When we are wounded by sin or weakness, the resurrected Savior has power to heal us completely as we return to Him.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- [Matthew 4:19](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/matt/4?lang=eng&id=p19#p19) — “Come, follow me.”
- [Matthew 11:28–30](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/matt/11?lang=eng&id=p28-p30#p28) — Christ invites the weary and heavy laden to come unto Him.
- [2 Nephi 26:33](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/2-ne/26?lang=eng&id=p33#p33) — The Lord invites all to come unto Him and denies none.
- [3 Nephi 27:21](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/3-ne/27?lang=eng&id=p21#p21) — “That which ye have seen me do even that shall ye do.”
- [Romans 3:23](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/rom/3?lang=eng&id=p23#p23) — All have sinned and fall short of the glory of God.
- [3 Nephi 9:13–14](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/3-ne/9?lang=eng&id=p13-p14#p13) — Return, repent, be converted, and Christ will heal you.
- The covenant path as the path of discipleship centered on Jesus Christ.
- The Atonement of Jesus Christ as both redeeming and healing power.
- President Russell M. Nelson’s teaching that Jesus Christ took upon Himself our individual sins, pains, heartaches, and infirmities.
- President Dallin H. Oaks’s declaration: “Jesus Christ is the way!”

### Questions to Ponder

- What are some “spiritual land mines” in today’s world that may be hard to see without Christ’s guidance?
- How can I more deliberately walk in the Savior’s footsteps this week?
- What experiences have helped me develop trust in Jesus Christ as my personal guide?
- How do ordinances and covenants connect me more closely to the Savior?
- Where do I need the Savior’s healing power in my life right now?
- How can I help others know that Christ’s invitation is extended to all and that He denies none?
