### Summary

Elder Wakolo invites all—especially part-member families and waiting loved ones—to come unto Christ together through patient faith, sacred agency, temple covenants, and trust in God’s timing.

### Haiku

Patient hearts still pray
Temple light gathers families
Christ invites us home

### Key Points

1. God’s plan centers on bringing His children to eternal life as families through Jesus Christ and temple covenants.
2. Conversion is personal, agency is sacred, and loving invitations should reflect the Savior’s patience and gentleness.
3. The Lord honors faithful waiting and keeps His promises in His own timing, bringing miracles to families one by one.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- [Moses 1:31–39](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/pgp/moses/1?lang=eng&id=p31-p39#p31) — God’s work and glory is to bring to pass immortality and eternal life.
- [2 Nephi 26:33](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/2-ne/26?lang=eng&id=p33#p33) — The Lord invites all to come unto Him and denies none.
- [Doctrine and Covenants 49:15–17](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/49?lang=eng&id=p15-p17#p15) — Marriage is ordained of God and fulfills the purpose of creation.
- [Doctrine and Covenants 2:3](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/2?lang=eng&id=p3#p3) / [Malachi 4:6](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/mal/4?lang=eng&id=p6#p6) — Without sealing authority, the earth would be wasted at the Lord’s coming.
- President Dallin H. Oaks’s teaching that the doctrine of the Church centers on the family and temple ordinances enable eternal families.
- Temple sealing as the crowning ordinance and a symbol of hope, not pressure.
- Christlike ministering one by one so that no one sits alone.

### Questions to Ponder

- How can I invite loved ones to come unto Christ without pressure or impatience?
- What does it mean to honor agency while still exercising faith in God’s promises?
- How can the temple become a symbol of hope and unity in my family?
- Who in my ward, family, or community may be waiting, praying, or sitting alone?
- What commitment can I make now to come unto Christ together with others?
