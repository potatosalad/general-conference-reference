### Summary

President Christofferson teaches that preparing for the Savior’s return means receiving a mighty change of heart so our motivations—and therefore our character—become like Jesus Christ’s.

### Haiku

Changed hearts turn outward
Pure love lifts, serves, and obeys
Christ forms us within

### Key Points

1. Christlike character grows from Christlike motivations—the thoughts, desires, and intents of the heart.
2. The Savior’s character is rooted in pure love, ministering to others, and complete devotion to the will of the Father.
3. Through faith, repentance, covenants, and the Holy Ghost, we can receive grace to become the people God needs us to be.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- [2 Peter 3:3–14](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/2-pet/3?lang=eng&id=p3-p14#p3) — preparing for the day of the Lord in holiness and peace
- [Mosiah 5:2–7](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/mosiah/5?lang=eng&id=p2-p7#p2) — King Benjamin’s people receive a mighty change of heart and take upon themselves the name of Christ
- [Moroni 7:47–48](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/moro/7?lang=eng&id=p47-p48#p47) — charity as the pure love of Christ
- [John 15:13](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/john/15?lang=eng&id=p13#p13) — the greatest love is to lay down one’s life for others
- [Moses 1:39](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/pgp/moses/1?lang=eng&id=p39#p39) — God’s work and glory: immortality and eternal life
- [Matthew 20:26–28](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/matt/20?lang=eng&id=p26-p28#p26) — the Son of Man came to minister and give His life
- [Matthew 11:29](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/matt/11?lang=eng&id=p29#p29) — learn of Christ, who is meek and lowly in heart
- [John 4:34](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/john/4?lang=eng&id=p34#p34) — Christ’s desire to do the Father’s will
- [Hebrews 4:15](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/heb/4?lang=eng&id=p15#p15) — Christ was tempted in all points yet without sin
- President Russell M. Nelson’s invitation to become the people God needs us to be

### Questions to Ponder

- What desires or motivations currently shape my character?
- How can I invite the Holy Ghost to help me experience a mighty change of heart?
- When I am suffering, how can I turn outward in Christlike love rather than inward?
- What opportunities do I have to lift and minister to others more intentionally?
- In what ways can I better align my thoughts, words, and actions with the will of God?
- How does becoming more Christlike help prepare the world for the Savior’s Second Coming?
