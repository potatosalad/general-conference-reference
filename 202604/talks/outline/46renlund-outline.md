### Summary

Elder Renlund teaches that focusing on Jesus Christ and His infinite Atonement brings clarity, joy, forgiveness, healing, and hope amid life’s many overwhelming concerns.

### Haiku

Christ rose from the tomb
Broken hearts find peace in Him
Joy swallows sorrow

### Key Points

1. Jesus Christ’s Atonement is central to Heavenly Father’s plan and gives us eternal perspective when life feels overwhelming.
2. Because of Jesus Christ, resurrection is universal and forgiveness is available to all who sincerely repent and follow Him.
3. The Savior perfectly understands our pains and can make all unfairness right, consecrating affliction for our gain.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- [John 14:6](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/john/14?lang=eng&id=p6#p6) — Jesus Christ is the way, the truth, and the life.
- [Matthew 28:18](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/matt/28?lang=eng&id=p18#p18) — The risen Christ has all power in heaven and earth.
- [2 Nephi 2:8](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/2-ne/2?lang=eng&id=p8#p8) — Salvation comes through the merits, mercy, and grace of the Holy Messiah.
- [Alma 34:31](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/alma/34?lang=eng&id=p31#p31) — Through repentance, the plan of redemption is immediately brought about unto us.
- [Doctrine and Covenants 58:42–43](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/58?lang=eng&id=p42-p43#p42) — The Lord forgives and remembers sins no more when we repent.
- [Alma 7:11–13](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/alma/7?lang=eng&id=p11-p13#p11) — Christ took upon Himself pains, sicknesses, and infirmities so He could succor His people.
- [2 Nephi 2:2](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/2-ne/2?lang=eng&id=p2#p2) — The Lord can consecrate afflictions for our gain.
- President Russell M. Nelson’s teaching that the answer is always found in the life and teachings of Jesus Christ.
- President Dallin H. Oaks’s declaration: “Jesus Christ is the way.”
- Gospel principles: the Atonement of Jesus Christ, repentance, resurrection, covenant discipleship, eternal perspective, healing through Christ.

### Questions to Ponder

- What “balls” in my life are distracting me from what matters most eternally?
- How can I more intentionally focus on Jesus Christ each day?
- What does it mean to me that resurrection is a universal and unconditional gift?
- Do I truly believe that sincere repentance leaves no spiritual scar, track, or trace?
- How has the Savior calmed my anguish or made something unfair in my life right?
- What blessings have I received today because of Jesus Christ?
