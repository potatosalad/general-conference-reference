### Summary

Elder Gong teaches that the resurrected Jesus Christ walks with us on our personal roads to Emmaus through scripture, sacrament, covenants, the Holy Ghost, and loving fellowship.

### Haiku

Dusty roads grow bright
Bread is broken, hearts now burn
Christ walks by our side

### Key Points

1. The Emmaus story shows that Christ may be near even when we do not at first recognize Him.
2. Through scriptures, sacrament, covenants, and the Holy Ghost, we can abide in Christ and know He lives.
3. Disciples of Christ help ensure that no one walks alone, unseen, or unsupported.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- [Luke 24:13–35](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/luke/24?lang=eng&id=p13-p35#p13) — The road to Emmaus and recognizing Christ in the breaking of bread
- [Mark 16:9–14](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/mark/16?lang=eng&id=p9-p14#p9) — The risen Lord appears to Mary Magdalene, two disciples, and the Eleven
- [John 14](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/john/14?lang=eng) — The First Comforter, the Holy Ghost, and the promise that Christ will come to His disciples
- [John 15](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/john/15?lang=eng) — Christ as the true vine and the invitation to abide in Him
- Sacrament covenants — taking Christ’s name, always remembering Him, keeping His commandments, and receiving His Spirit
- Solemn assembly and sustaining living prophets
- [2 Nephi 11:5](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/2-ne/11?lang=eng&id=p5#p5) — Delighting in the Lord’s covenants, grace, justice, power, and mercy
- The Atonement and Resurrection of Jesus Christ, including healing, reconciliation, covenant belonging, and eternal families

### Questions to Ponder

- When have I realized, perhaps later, that the Savior had been walking with me?
- How can scripture study and the sacrament help my heart “burn” with testimony of Christ?
- What does it mean for me personally to abide in Christ and have Him abide in me?
- Who around me may be walking a lonely road to Emmaus, and how can I walk with them?
- How can I better sustain and follow the Lord’s living prophet with willingness, obedience, and joy?
