### Summary

Elder Eduardo F. Ortega teaches that true discipleship requires each person—whether from a long-standing Church family or newly baptized—to seek a personal testimony of Jesus Christ and steadily press forward on the covenant path.

### Haiku

Small steps toward the Light
Faith becomes our own in Christ
He completes the climb

### Key Points

1. Faith is not inherited; every disciple must personally seek and receive a testimony of Jesus Christ.
2. Family heritage can bless and strengthen us, but it cannot replace individual conversion and covenant keeping.
3. Lifelong discipleship is built through small daily spiritual choices as we rely on Christ, the author and finisher of our faith.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- [Matthew 16:17](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/matt/16?lang=eng&id=p17#p17) — testimony comes by revelation from Heavenly Father, not “flesh and blood.”
- [Alma 32](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/alma/32?lang=eng) — experimenting upon the word and nurturing faith.
- [2 Nephi 31:19–20](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/2-ne/31?lang=eng&id=p19-p20#p19) — pressing forward with steadfastness in Christ and enduring to the end.
- [Moroni 6:4](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/moro/6?lang=eng&id=p4#p4) — being nourished by the good word of God and relying on Christ’s merits.
- [Hebrews 12:2](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/heb/12?lang=eng&id=p2#p2) — Jesus Christ as the author and finisher of our faith.
- President Dallin H. Oaks’s teaching that building faith and overcoming doubt can be a lengthy process.
- The covenant path, personal conversion, enduring to the end, and relying wholly on the merits of Jesus Christ.

### Questions to Ponder

- How have I come to know for myself that Jesus Christ lives?
- In what ways might I be relying on family, culture, or tradition instead of nurturing my own testimony?
- What small daily spiritual choices are helping me become a lifelong disciple of Christ?
- How can I better welcome and strengthen those who are new to the Church?
- When have I seen the Savior’s refining and perfecting hand in my life over time?
