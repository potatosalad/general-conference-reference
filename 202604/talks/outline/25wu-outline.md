### Summary

Elder Wan-Liang Wu teaches that through sincere faith, repentance, and willingness to give up our sins, we can come to know Heavenly Father and receive eternal life through Jesus Christ.

### Haiku

Sins laid at His feet
Christ shows the Father’s bright face
Joy blooms through changed hearts

### Key Points

1. Knowing God requires more than learning about Him; it requires faith in Jesus Christ, repentance, and covenant action.
2. Like the converted king in the Book of Mormon, we must be willing to give up anything—especially our sins—to truly know God.
3. Jesus Christ is the only way to the Father, and following Him brings happiness, hope, peace, purpose, and eternal life.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- [Alma 22](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/alma/22?lang=eng): Aaron teaches King Lamoni’s father about God, the Fall, the plan of redemption, faith, and repentance.
- [Alma 22:18](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/alma/22?lang=eng&id=p18#p18): “I will give away all my sins to know thee.”
- [John 17:3](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/john/17?lang=eng&id=p3#p3): Eternal life is knowing the only true God and Jesus Christ, whom He sent.
- [John 14:6](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/john/14?lang=eng&id=p6#p6): Jesus Christ is “the way, the truth, and the life.”
- [2 Nephi 31:21](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/2-ne/31?lang=eng&id=p21#p21): There is no other way or name given whereby salvation can come.
- President Dallin H. Oaks’s teaching that repentance means giving up practices contrary to God’s commandments and becoming “celestial citizens.”
- Gospel principles of faith in Jesus Christ, repentance, conversion, obedience, and coming unto Christ.

### Questions to Ponder

- What have I learned about Heavenly Father by coming unto Jesus Christ?
- What sins, habits, or attitudes might I need to give away in order to know God more fully?
- How can I move from simply learning about Christ to truly following Him?
- When have I felt the gospel give me greater happiness, hope, peace, or purpose?
- How can sincere desire and real intent make my prayers and repentance more meaningful?
