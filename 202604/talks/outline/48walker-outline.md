### Summary

Elder Walker teaches that those who obey the Savior’s voice and faithfully keep their covenants become the Lord’s “peculiar treasure,” receiving His acceptance, strength, and covenant blessings.

### Haiku

Covenants bind hearts
Treasured by the Living Christ
We rise through His love

### Key Points

1. The Lord invites His people to become His peculiar treasure by obeying His voice and keeping His covenants.
2. Covenant faithfulness brings divine power, closeness to God, and strength to rise above the world.
3. The Lord accepts honest, broken, and contrite hearts that are willing to sacrifice in order to honor covenants.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- [Exodus 19:5](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/ex/19?lang=eng&id=p5#p5) — becoming a peculiar treasure by obeying God’s voice and keeping His covenant
- [Moses 1:39](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/pgp/moses/1?lang=eng&id=p39#p39) — God’s work and glory is our immortality and eternal life
- [John 15:13](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/john/15?lang=eng&id=p13#p13) — the Savior’s supreme love shown through His sacrifice
- [Doctrine and Covenants 97:8](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/97?lang=eng&id=p8#p8) — honest hearts, broken spirits, and covenant sacrifice are accepted of the Lord
- President Russell M. Nelson’s teachings on the covenant relationship with God
- President Dallin H. Oaks’s teachings on honoring covenants and rising to celestial heights
- President D. Todd Christofferson’s teachings on covenant blessings and divine power

- What sacrifices might the Lord be inviting me to make so I can keep my covenants more fully?
