### Summary

President Oaks testified that Jesus Christ is the way to peace and eternal life, inviting members to study conference messages, act in faith, strengthen families, and receive personal revelation.

### Haiku

Christ calls us to peace
Families shine with His love
Look to God and live

### Key Points

1. Jesus Christ is central to Heavenly Father’s plan and is the source of peace, eternal life, and joy.
2. Disciples of Christ should minister with charity and show pure love in families, communities, and all interactions.
3. Studying and acting on conference teachings brings greater light, personal revelation, and divine guidance.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- [John 14:6](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/john/14?lang=eng&id=p6#p6) — Jesus Christ is the way, the truth, and the life.
- [Moses 6:33–34](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/pgp/moses/6?lang=eng&id=p33-p34#p33) — “Look unto me, and endure to the end, and ye shall live.”
- [Alma 37:47](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/alma/37?lang=eng&id=p47#p47) — “Look to God and live.”
- [Doctrine and Covenants 50:24](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/50?lang=eng&id=p24#p24) — Those who receive light and continue in God receive more light.
- [Doctrine and Covenants 84:85](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/84?lang=eng&id=p85#p85) — Treasure up words of life and receive guidance in the moment needed.
- The doctrine of Christlike ministering one by one with charity and love.
- The central role of marriage and family in Heavenly Father’s plan of salvation.

### Questions to Ponder

- How can I more fully make Jesus Christ the center of my worship, learning, and testimony?
- What conference messages do I feel prompted to study again and act upon?
- How can I demonstrate the pure love of Christ more consistently in my family and community?
- What does it mean for me personally to “look to God and live”?
- How have I received more light or revelation when I acted on prophetic counsel?
