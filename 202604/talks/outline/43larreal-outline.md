### Summary

Elder Larreal teaches that as we prepare for and partake of the sacrament reverently, we can feel the Savior’s personal love and receive the constant companionship of the Holy Ghost.

### Haiku

Broken bread, pure love
His Spirit rests on our souls
We remember Him

### Key Points

1. The sacrament is a sacred weekly opportunity to remember Jesus Christ and His Atonement personally.
2. Partaking with a broken heart and contrite spirit helps us qualify for the constant companionship of the Holy Ghost.
3. Reverence and spiritual preparation during the sacrament help us feel the Savior’s love and receive strength against deception and temptation.
