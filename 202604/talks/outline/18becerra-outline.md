### Summary

Elder Jorge T. Becerra teaches that paying tithes and offerings helps us overcome double-mindedness, put God first, and receive greater spiritual power and direction.

### Haiku

Firstlings humbly given
Heaven’s windows fill with light
God first, hearts made whole

### Key Points

1. Putting God first helps disciples avoid the instability of being spiritually double-minded.
2. Tithing and offerings are modern ways to offer our “firstlings” to the Lord in faith and obedience.
3. Obedience to the law of tithing brings spiritual illumination, direction, and increased capacity.
