### Summary

Elder Michael John U. Teh teaches that following living prophets helps us follow Jesus Christ, embrace gospel culture, receive strength through the Atonement, and reflect the Savior’s light to others.

### Haiku

Prophets point to Christ
Covenants reshape our hearts
His light shines through us

### Key Points

1. Following prophets, seers, and revelators is a way of following the Savior Jesus Christ.
2. True discipleship may require changing personal, family, or cultural traditions that conflict with gospel covenants.
3. Through the Atonement of Jesus Christ, we can receive strength to endure trials and become a light to others.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- [Luke 18:22](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/luke/18?lang=eng&id=p22#p22) — Jesus invites the rich young ruler to sacrifice and follow Him.
- [Matthew 6:20](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/matt/6?lang=eng&id=p20#p20) — Treasures in heaven are eternal and incorruptible.
- [Matthew 5:14–16](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/matt/5?lang=eng&id=p14-p16#p14) — Disciples are the light of the world.
- [3 Nephi 18:24](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/3-ne/18?lang=eng&id=p24#p24) — Hold up the light of Christ through our example.
- [Mosiah 8:17](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/mosiah/8?lang=eng&id=p17#p17) — Seers can know things past and future and reveal what otherwise could not be known.
- President Dallin H. Oaks’s teachings on continuous discipleship, repentance, gospel culture, and the strengthening power of the Savior’s Atonement.
- The doctrine of living prophets, seers, and revelators as watchmen who guide the Church through revelation.

### Questions to Ponder

- What does it mean for me to follow Christ as a continuous commitment rather than an occasional practice?
- Are there traditions, habits, or cultural expectations in my life that need to be brought more fully in line with gospel culture?
- How have living prophets helped me see the path ahead more clearly?
- When have I felt the strengthening and enabling power of the Atonement of Jesus Christ during difficulty?
- How can I better reflect the Savior’s light so others feel His love through me?
