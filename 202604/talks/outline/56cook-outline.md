### Summary

Elder Cook testifies that through Jesus Christ’s Atonement and Resurrection and the restored priesthood keys of sealing, God’s children can make and keep covenants that unite families eternally and bring them back to the Father.

### Haiku

Easter morning light
Keys turn hearts across all time
Families sealed in Christ

### Key Points

1. Jesus Christ’s Atonement and Resurrection make salvation and eternal life possible through repentance and covenant faithfulness.
2. Elijah restored the sealing keys in the Kirtland Temple, enabling temple ordinances that bind families on earth and in heaven.
3. We can receive spiritual confirmation of Christ, the Restoration, and eternal families through scripture study, temple worship, and keeping covenants.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles
