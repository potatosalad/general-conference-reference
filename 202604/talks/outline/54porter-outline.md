### Summary

President Susan H. Porter teaches children that, because of Heavenly Father’s plan and Jesus Christ’s Atonement, they can offer their gifts and say, “Here am I, send me,” to help bring others to Christ.

### Haiku

Small loaves in His hands
Children shine with covenant light
Jesus walks with them

### Key Points

1. Each child is a beloved spirit child of God with gifts, purpose, and power to do good.
2. Jesus Christ volunteered to be our Savior, overcame sin and death, and walks with us as we follow Him.
3. When we offer what we have, even if it seems small, Heavenly Father can use it to bless others now and eternally.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- [Isaiah 6:8](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/isa/6?lang=eng&id=p8#p8) — “Here am I; send me.”
- [Abraham 3:27](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/pgp/abr/3?lang=eng&id=p27#p27) — Jesus Christ’s premortal willingness to fulfill the Father’s plan.
- [John 6:5–13](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/john/6?lang=eng&id=p5-p13#p5) — Jesus feeds the five thousand with five loaves and two fishes.
- [3 Nephi 17](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/3-ne/17?lang=eng) — The resurrected Savior blesses the children.
- [Doctrine and Covenants 64:33](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/64?lang=eng&id=p33#p33) — “By small and simple things are great things brought to pass.”
- The plan of salvation and our premortal identity as children of Heavenly Parents.
- The Atonement and Resurrection of Jesus Christ.
- Covenant children have gifts to give in gathering Israel and blessing others.

### Questions to Ponder

- What gifts has Heavenly Father given me that I can offer to Him?
- When have I seen a small act of kindness become a blessing to someone else?
- How can I say “Here am I, send me” in my prayers this week?
- Who might Heavenly Father be sending me to comfort, help, or invite closer to Jesus Christ?
- How does knowing I lived with God before this life change the way I see myself and others?
