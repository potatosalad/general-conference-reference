### Summary

Elder Neil L. Andersen teaches that eternal marriage, made possible through Christ’s Resurrection and restored sealing power, is a sacred covenant journey of becoming more like the Savior through faith, patience, sacrifice, and enduring love.

### Haiku

Hands clasped at the veil
Covenants weather all storms
Love walks toward the dawn

### Key Points

1. The sealing power restored through Jesus Christ binds faithful spouses and families together eternally.
2. Eternal marriage is not merely a ceremony but a lifelong and eternal journey of spiritual refinement and Christlike becoming.
3. Couples need the Holy Ghost, patience, repentance, forgiveness, and hope in Christ to navigate trials and remain faithful to covenants.

### Questions to Ponder

- How does the Savior’s Resurrection make eternal marriage and eternal families possible?
- In what ways can marriage become a “crucible of spiritual development”?
- What Christlike attributes is the Lord helping me develop through my closest relationships?
- How can couples keep their temple covenants central during times of disappointment, illness, or sorrow?
- Why is the influence of the Holy Ghost essential in marriage and family life?
- How can hope in Jesus Christ strengthen those who are not yet sealed or whose family circumstances are difficult?
