### Summary

Elder Chi Hong (Sam) Wong teaches that the Book of Mormon’s repeated invitation to “remember, remember” points us to obedience, agency, covenants, and salvation through Jesus Christ.

### Haiku

Remember the Rock
Christ rises, redeems, and leads
Choose His joy today

### Key Points

1. Keeping God’s commandments brings temporal and spiritual blessings and leads to eternal happiness with God.
2. Salvation comes only through the atoning blood of Jesus Christ, who is central to Heavenly Father’s plan.
3. We must use agency wisely and build our lives on Christ through covenants, ordinances, and faithful remembrance.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- [Mosiah 2:41](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/mosiah/2?lang=eng&id=p41#p41) — the blessed and happy state of those who keep the commandments
- [Alma 37:13](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/alma/37?lang=eng&id=p13#p13) — obedience brings prosperity; disobedience cuts us off from God’s presence
- [Helaman 5:9](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/hel/5?lang=eng&id=p9#p9) — salvation comes only through the atoning blood of Jesus Christ
- [Helaman 5:12](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/hel/5?lang=eng&id=p12#p12) — building on the rock of Christ creates a sure foundation
- [Helaman 14:30](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/hel/14?lang=eng&id=p30#p30) — God has made us free to act for ourselves
- [Job 38:7](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/job/38?lang=eng&id=p7#p7) — we rejoiced in Heavenly Father’s plan before this life
- The Resurrection and redeeming mission of Jesus Christ
- Moral agency and accountability
- Sacred covenants and ordinances as spiritual anchors to Christ

### Questions to Ponder

- What does the Lord most want me to “remember, remember” right now?
- How have God’s commandments blessed and steadied my life?
- In what ways am I building my foundation on Jesus Christ?
- How can remembering my covenants help me withstand spiritual storms?
- How can I use my agency more wisely each day to come unto Christ?
