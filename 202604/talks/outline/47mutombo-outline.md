### Summary

Elder Mutombo teaches that through Jesus Christ’s Atonement and Resurrection, covenant faithfulness brings divine love, strength in sorrow, and lasting joy with God.

### Haiku

Christ is truly risen
Covenants hold through sorrow
Sunday joy will come

### Key Points

1. Jesus Christ’s Atonement and Resurrection offer hope, peace, and the promise of eternal life to all who come unto Him.
2. Covenant relationships with God and family can give strength to stand faithful even amid grief, cultural pressure, and unanswered prayers.
3. Keeping covenants requires lifelong discipleship, obedience, humility, and faith that God’s promised joy will come.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- [3 Nephi 9:14](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/3-ne/9?lang=eng&id=p14#p14) — the Savior’s invitation to come unto Him and receive eternal life
- [Doctrine and Covenants 121](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/121?lang=eng) — Joseph Smith’s pleading in Liberty Jail during deep suffering
- [Luke 16:15](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/luke/16?lang=eng&id=p15#p15) and [Mark 7:8](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/mark/7?lang=eng&id=p8#p8) — leaving the praise and traditions of men to follow God
- [2 Nephi 2:27](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/2-ne/2?lang=eng&id=p27#p27) — Satan seeks that all might be miserable like unto himself
- [Helaman 5:12](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/hel/5?lang=eng&id=p12#p12) — building upon the rock of Jesus Christ
- Baptismal and temple covenants as sources of divine love, identity, and strength
- President Dallin H. Oaks’s teaching that following Christ is a continuous commitment and way of life
- President Ezra Taft Benson’s teaching on obedience, doing the Lord’s will, and loving God

### Questions to Ponder

- How have your covenants helped you feel God’s love during difficult times?
- What does “Come unto me” mean for you in your current circumstances?
- When have you had to choose between the Lord’s way and the traditions or expectations of others?
- How can remembering the Resurrection help you trust that “Sunday will come”?
- What is one way you can strengthen your covenant relationship with Heavenly Father and Jesus Christ this week?
