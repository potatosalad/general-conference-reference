### Summary

President Emily Belle Freeman teaches that as we walk with Jesus Christ through both best and worst days, eternal truths, divine invitations, and promised blessings strengthen us and assure us our best days are ahead.

### Haiku

Storm winds rise and fall
Christ stands near with outstretched hand
Joy waits in His light

### Key Points

1. Mortality includes both best and worst days, and God uses both to build testimony and growth.
2. Like Peter, we gain strength by responding to Christ’s invitations and reaching for Him when we feel afraid or inadequate.
3. Because of Jesus Christ’s Atonement and Resurrection, His promises will be fulfilled and our future can be filled with hope.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- [Matthew 16:13–19](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/matt/16?lang=eng&id=p13-p19#p13) — Peter’s testimony that Jesus is the Christ
- [Matthew 14:22–33](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/matt/14?lang=eng&id=p22-p33#p22) — Peter walks on water toward the Savior
- [Luke 22:31–34, 54–62](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/luke/22?lang=eng&id=p31-p34,p54-p62#p31) — Christ prays for Peter and Peter denies Him
- [John 21:15–17](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/john/21?lang=eng&id=p15-p17#p15) — The risen Lord invites Peter to feed His sheep
- For the Strength of Youth: A Guide for Making Choices — eternal truths, invitations, and promised blessings
- The doctrine of Jesus Christ as our source of strength in trials
- The principle that joy is the presence of Jesus Christ, not the absence of sorrow
- Temple ordinances and covenants provide greater access to God’s blessings

### Questions to Ponder

- When have you experienced a best day turning into a worst day, and how did the Savior help you?
- What eternal truths have been strengthened in you over time through both joyful and difficult experiences?
- What invitation from the Lord feels too big right now, and how can you remember He is within reaching distance?
- How might Christ’s look toward Peter after his denial be seen as a look of love and enabling strength?
- How can For the Strength of Youth become not only a guide for standards but a guide for strength in your life?
- What does it mean to you that because of Jesus Christ, your best days are ahead?
