### Summary

President D. Todd Christofferson presented releases, new callings, and sustaining votes for General Authorities, Area Seventies, and General Officers of the Church, inviting members to express support and gratitude.

### Haiku

Hands rise in covenant
Servants called, thanked, and sustained
One Church moves forward

### Key Points

1. Sustaining leaders is a sacred opportunity for Church members to show faith, unity, and support.
2. Releases from Church callings are occasions to express gratitude for consecrated service by leaders and their families.
3. The work of the Church continues through orderly succession, inspired callings, and worldwide service.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- [Doctrine and Covenants 26:2](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/26?lang=eng&id=p2#p2) — all things shall be done by common consent in the Church
- [Doctrine and Covenants 107:22](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/107?lang=eng&id=p22#p22) — the First Presidency is upheld by the confidence, faith, and prayer of the Church
- [Doctrine and Covenants 124:144](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/124?lang=eng&id=p144#p144) — officers are to be approved by the voice of the Church
- [Articles of Faith 1:5](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/pgp/a-of-f/1?lang=eng&id=p5#p5) — individuals are called of God by prophecy and by the laying on of hands by those in authority
