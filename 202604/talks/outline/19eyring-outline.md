### Summary

President Eyring teaches that continual, heartfelt prayer invites the Savior’s promised peace and strengthening support in every circumstance.

### Haiku

Hearts rise silently
Peace descends through faithful prayer
Christ calms troubled souls

### Key Points

1. The Savior offers peace that is deeper and more lasting than anything the world can give.
2. Prayer need not be eloquent or lengthy; God hears sincere prayers from the heart, even unspoken ones.
3. Consistent prayer in both joy and hardship brings spiritual strength, revelation, and the ability to bear burdens.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- [John 14:27](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/john/14?lang=eng&id=p27#p27) — “Peace I leave with you, my peace I give unto you.”
- [Psalm 9:9–10](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/ps/9?lang=eng&id=p9-p10#p9) — The Lord is a refuge in times of trouble.
- [Luke 11:9–10](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/luke/11?lang=eng&id=p9-p10#p9) — Ask, seek, and knock.
- [Matthew 6:6–8](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/matt/6?lang=eng&id=p6-p8#p6) — Pray sincerely, not with vain repetitions.
- [Mosiah 24:12–15](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/mosiah/24?lang=eng&id=p12-p15#p12) — Alma’s people pray in their hearts and receive strength to bear burdens.
- [Alma 17:3](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/alma/17?lang=eng&id=p3#p3) — The sons of Mosiah receive spiritual power through prayer and fasting.
- [Alma 7:27](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/alma/7?lang=eng&id=p27#p27) — The peace of God resting upon homes and families.
- Principle of praying always and having hearts drawn out continually to God.

### Questions to Ponder

- How have you felt the Savior’s peace in answer to prayer?
- What does it mean to have a continual prayer in your heart?
- How can sincere prayer help us when burdens are not immediately removed?
- Why is it important to pray consistently before moments of crisis arise?
- How can we make personal and family prayers more heartfelt?
