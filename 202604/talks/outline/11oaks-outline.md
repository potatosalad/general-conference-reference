### Summary

President Dallin H. Oaks welcomes members to a unique general conference beginning with a solemn assembly to sustain newly called Church leaders and reaffirm faith in the resurrected Jesus Christ, the head of His Church.

### Haiku

Saints raise hands in faith
Christ leads His Church from heaven
Easter hope shines bright

### Key Points

1. A solemn assembly is a sacred occasion in which members sustain the First Presidency and Quorum of the Twelve Apostles by quorums and groups.
2. Sustaining Church leaders includes offering prayers, support, and faithful service in God’s work throughout the world.
3. Jesus Christ is the resurrected Lord and the head of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- [Doctrine and Covenants 107:22](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/107?lang=eng&id=p22#p22) — The First Presidency is upheld by the confidence, faith, and prayers of the Church.
- [Doctrine and Covenants 26:2](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/26?lang=eng&id=p2#p2) — All things in the Church are to be done by common consent.
- [Doctrine and Covenants 1:38](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/1?lang=eng&id=p38#p38) — Whether by the Lord’s voice or the voice of His servants, it is the same.
- [Mosiah 18:8–10](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/mosiah/18?lang=eng&id=p8-p10#p8) — Covenants include willingness to serve and stand as witnesses of God.
- The principle of sustaining those called by revelation to preside in the Church.
- The doctrine that Jesus Christ is the resurrected Savior and the head of His Church.

### Questions to Ponder

- What does it mean to truly sustain Church leaders beyond raising a hand?
- How can my prayers and service strengthen the work of the Lord worldwide?
- Why is it significant that Jesus Christ, not any mortal leader, is the head of the Church?
- How does the Easter season deepen my testimony of the resurrected Lord?
