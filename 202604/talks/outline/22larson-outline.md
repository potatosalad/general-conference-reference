### Summary

The Church Auditing Department reported that 2025 Church contributions, expenditures, and assets were audited and administered according to approved budgets, policies, and sound financial principles.

### Haiku

Sacred funds guarded
Faithful stewards count with care
Trust rests in the Lord

### Key Points

1. The Council on the Disposition of the Tithes authorizes Church expenditures as directed by revelation in [Doctrine and Covenants 120](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/120?lang=eng).
2. Church Auditing operates independently to provide reasonable assurance that contributions, expenditures, and assets are properly recorded and safeguarded.
3. The Church follows principles taught to members: living within a budget, avoiding debt, and saving for future needs.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- [Doctrine and Covenants 120](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/120?lang=eng) — the Council on the Disposition of the Tithes
- [Doctrine and Covenants 104:11–18](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/104?lang=eng&id=p11-p18#p11) — stewardship and accountability
- The law of tithing and sacred use of consecrated funds
- Financial self-reliance: budgeting, avoiding unnecessary debt, and saving
- Honesty, accountability, and wise stewardship in the Lord’s work
