### Summary

Elder Holmes teaches that Jesus Christ is both our Guide and the only covenant path home, and we stay safely bound to Him by keeping covenants and following His living prophet.

### Haiku

Steep mountain pathway
Bound to Christ, we walk with light
Prophets guide us home

### Key Points

1. Jesus Christ, through His Atonement, is the only way to return to Heavenly Father and receive eternal life.
2. Covenants bind us to the Savior and give us access to His strength, peace, joy, and power as we remain faithful.
3. Following Jesus Christ includes hearkening to His living prophets, who keep us anchored in true doctrine and safe from spiritual confusion.

### Scriptures and Gospel Principles

- [John 14:6](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/john/14?lang=eng&id=p6#p6) — Jesus Christ is “the way, the truth, and the life.”
- [John 3:5](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/john/3?lang=eng&id=p5#p5) — We must be born of water and of the Spirit to enter God’s kingdom.
- [John 6:66–69](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/john/6?lang=eng&id=p66-p69#p66) — Peter’s testimony: “Lord, to whom shall we go?”
- [Luke 10:16](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/luke/10?lang=eng&id=p16#p16) — Those who hear the Lord’s chosen servants hear Him.
- [Doctrine and Covenants 1:16](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/1?lang=eng&id=p16#p16) — Warning against every person walking in their own way.
- [Ephesians 2:20](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/eph/2?lang=eng&id=p20#p20) and 4:11–14 — The Church is built on apostles and prophets, with Christ as the chief cornerstone.
- [Doctrine and Covenants 124:45](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/124?lang=eng&id=p45#p45) — Those who hearken to the Lord and His appointed servants “shall not be moved out of their place.”
- The covenant path: faith in Jesus Christ, repentance, ordinances, covenant keeping, and enduring to the end.
- President Dallin H. Oaks’s teaching that the strongest help God has given us is the Savior, Jesus Christ.
- Elder David A. Bednar’s teaching that covenant connection is a source of perspective, hope, power, peace, and enduring joy.

### Questions to Ponder

- When have I felt that I needed the Savior to guide me through a steep or difficult part of life?
- How do my covenants help bind me more closely to Jesus Christ?
- What might cause someone to loosen or sever their covenant connection with the Savior?
- How can I respond with Peter’s faith when discipleship feels demanding or when others turn away?
- In what ways does following the living prophet help me follow Jesus Christ more fully?
- What is one step I can take now to stay more firmly on the covenant path?
